Liv Tyler's Changing Looks

June 2024 Â· 3 minute read

See the mom-of-three's transformation through the years.

1980

The 3-year-old Liv Tyler attended a photo shoot with her mother, model Bebe Buell.

1996

The fresh-faced newcomer hit the Cannes Film Festival to promote Stealing Beauty. "Here's one trick I learned on the set," she told InStyle. "After applying foundation and a little blush, dust your face with translucent powder, then mist skin with a rosewater spray and lay a Kleenex over it for a second. It makes your face seem flawless in a way that looks like you aren't wearing makeup."

1998

The Armageddon star had her locks chopped into a shaggy pixie: "My hair was down to my waist until the amazing stylist John Sahag cut off my ponytail."

1998

For the Costume Institute Gala, she styled her short crop into piecey fringe.

1999

The Cookie's Fortune star attended the Costume Institute Gala sporting a grown-out shag and a "Rock Royalty" tee courtesy of pal Stella McCartney.

2001

For the Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring premiere, she swept her heavy bangs to the side.

2003

She showcased her glowing skin with a chic, '60s-style updo.

2004

Tyler hit the SAG Awards in a deep red lipstick that heightened her natural coloring. Jersey Girl.

2004

For the Academy Awards, Tyler styled her long mane into a wavy faux-bob.

2006

A voluminous half-pompadour added interest to her long locks.

2008

She stopped by the MTV Movie Awards working a shoulder-length bob.

2010

The G-Star Raw model added a reddish tint to her long strands.

2011

Tyler played up her pout at a Super screening with cheerful pink lippy.

2012

At the Costume Institute Gala, Tyler rocked retro blunt bangs.

2014

At the 2014 Women's Leadership Award event honoring Stella McCartney, Tyler went with a touch of black eyeliner, a hint of color on her cheeks, and long side-parted locks.

2015

Tyler attended the opening night for the New York Spring Spectacular with a coral lip and her hair in loose curls.

2016

The expectant star rocked a bright pink lip and loose waves at the 21st Annual Critics' Choice Awards.

2017

The mom-of-three wore her dark hair in loose waves and minimal makeup, polishing off her look with mascara, eyeliner, and a glossy pink lip.
