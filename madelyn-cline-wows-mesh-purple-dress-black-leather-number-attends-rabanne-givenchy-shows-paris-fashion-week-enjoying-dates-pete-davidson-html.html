Madelyn Cline wows in mesh purple dress and black leather number as she attends Rabanne and Givenchy

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Madelyn Cline looked sensational as she attended the Rabanne and Givenchy shows during Paris Fashion Week on Thursday. 

The model, 25, started off the day wearing a purple figure semi- sheer hugging dress for Rabanne before changing into a one-shoulder leather number for Givenchy. 

Her lilac gown was patterned with autumnal coloured flowers while also featuring sections of lace detailing. 

The Outer Banks actress flashed her toned tummy through the mesh section of the dress as she cut an elegant figure in the garment. 

Changing style completely, Madelyn then changed into her black leather fitted dress which showed off her fabulous figure for her second show in one day.

The leather garment was striped with diagonal mesh stripes as she flashed her skin. 

Sweeping her hair back in a tight slick back bun, she paired the dress with some black lace up gloves, black pointed heels and rectangular shades.  

Her appearance at the event comes after it was reported that her and Pete Davidson, 29, are officially dating.   

The pair spent the night together in Las Vegas over the weekend as they headed to the city for the comedian's live stand up show at the Chelsea. 

Whilst away together they stayed in the Crockfords Entertainment Suite at Resorts World Las Vegas and reportedly spent the majority of their time at the hotel's Allē Lounge on 66, according to People. 

Sources also confirmed to the news outlet last Friday that the pair are now officially dating after rumours of a romance between the pair started circling. 

Their official status comes after it was reported earlier this week that Madelyn doesn't see the romance as 'serious'. 

An insider exclusively told DailyMail.com that while Madelyn is having 'fun' with the comedian, she doesn't believe the relationship will be more than 'a good time'.
