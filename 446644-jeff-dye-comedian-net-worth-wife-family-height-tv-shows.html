Jeff Dye's biography

Jeff was born on 4th February 1983 in Seattle, Washington DC, in the United States. The comedian is 39 years old as of 2022. He holds an American nationality and mixed-racial background. He is of Spanish, French-Canadian, German, and Jewish descent.

The actor's parents are John Dye (father) and Judy Dye (mother). There are no details about his parent's careers and current whereabouts. Even though he was born in Seattle, he was raised in Kent, Washington.

He has two siblings, Jennifer Dye-Ledbetter and Janice Lee Dye. Unfortunately, his elder sister, Janice, passed away on 3rd December 2015 in a car accident in Albany, Oregon. Jeff went to Kent High School in Covington. However, he did not learn to read until he was 20 years old since he had untreated dyslexia.
