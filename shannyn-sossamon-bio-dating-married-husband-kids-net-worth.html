Shannyn Sossamon Bio, Dating, Married, Husband, Kids, Net Worth

August 2024 · 7 minute read

US-born actress and musician Shannyn Sossamon is an iconic figure in the entertainment industry. Her performance as Jocelyn in 2001’s A Knight’s Tale has cemented her popularity among fans of the genre while her success both onscreen and offstage have made her a celebrated star. She has been honored with numerous awards that recognize not only her talent but also the rewarding career she has built over time which speaks to its outstanding quality.

Shannyn Sossamon Bio/Age

Shannyn Sossamon was born as Shannon Marie Kahololani Sossamon on 3 October, 1978 in Honolulu, Hawaii, United States. She was raised by her mother in Reno, Nevada, for most of her infant life. She is American and of English, French, Dutch, German and Irish descent.

Her maternal grandmother is also having Hawaiian and Filipino descent. Her father’s name is Todd Lindberg and her mother’s name is Sherry Sossamon, a nurse. She is the oldest of two daughters in the family. Her younger sister, Jenny Lee Lindberg, born in 1981, is a singer.

Shannyn and her sister were raised after their parents divorced their mother when she was just 5 years old and her sister was only 2 years old. Her mother then remarried Randy Goldman, who at that time worked as a manager of a car dealership and also as a salesman. Shannyn studied at Galena High School, where she also took dance lessons and graduated in 1995.

After graduating from high school she moved to Los Angeles and continued her dance studies there. Later she became a disc jockey (DJ) and was seen showing off her skills and performing at various venues, including several local clubs. At this point, she added the “Y” to her name to become “Shannyn” instead of “Shannon”.

Shannyn Sossamon Career

Shannyn Sossamon’s professional achievements are highly impressive. After graduating high school in the mid-1990s and relocating to Los Angeles she developed a strong passion for dancing. Despite continuing her studies in this art form she never intended it to become her career path but instead used it as an avenue of creative expression.

Later she developed an interest in modeling and was featured in several magazines including Planned Parenthood Sassy Magazine American Eagle Outfitters and Unionbay Clothing.

Her foray into television began with two commercials for GAP and music videos for 9xDead. She then went on to feature in music videos of renowned artists like Daft Punk and Cher furthering her career in the industry.

After appearing in various television shows such as the 1997 comedy series Mr. Show with Bob and David (her acting debut) she took a giant step forward with her portrayal of Lady Jocelyn in her first film “A Knight’s Tale” (2001). It is reported that she surpassed the renowned actress Kate Hudson in auditions for the female lead role in the film. Her performance earned her several award nominations including the MTV Movie Awards.

She appeared as Erica Sutton in the romantic comedy “40 Days and 40 Nights” (2002) by Miramax Films, but her big breakthrough came with her portrayal of Mikal in the 2006 love movie “Wristcutters”: A Love Story. Her performance in this film earned her numerous nominations for various awards. Among the other films in which she received recognition are several others: The Rules of Attraction (2002); The Order (2003); Devour (2005); One Missed Call (2008); Road to Nowhere (2010); The End of Love (2012); Sinister 2 (2015), among others.

Shannyn Sossamon Dating/Married

Shannyn Sossamon has found so many of her fans in her history who thought she deserved better. In 2002 Shannyn was involved in a romantic affair with Dallas Clayton, a writer, and illustrator, and by 2004 the couple had already split up again. But what seemed like a short affair produced a child – yes! The couple had a son together.

Audio Science Clayton was born on 29 May, 2003, and although Shannyn claimed at the time that she was not yet ready for the experience of motherhood, she succumbed to the situation and enjoyed every moment. She disappeared from the spotlight just to focus all her attention on taking care of her pregnancy and embracing motherhood.

Later in 2011, Shannyn Sossamon had another boy, Mortimer. Since then she has kept the information about the child’s father secret, like cards that are so close to her breast. But out of the blue, in 2015, she told Details magazine in an interview that she was “on the run” with the father of her second son.
