Full Pink Moon in Scorpio Will Affect Each Zodiac Sign 2024

May 2024 · 9 minute read

We’ve made it to the peak of springtime, and despite the abundance blooming from this flowery season, a transformative Pink Moon in Scorpio beckons you to face the ugly truth and embrace your shadow side. If you’re curious about how the full in Scorpio will affect each zodiac sign, it’s important to note that whatever is coming up for review is resurfacing for a reason. This fixed water sign is known for its intensity and unstoppable passion, not to mention its innate ability to uncover what’s beneath the surface. The Pink Moon isn’t always as pretty as it sounds, but Scorpio’s psychic radar will set you free.

With the sun journeying through the Venus-ruled sign of Taurus, themes surrounding our finances, values, and sense of security take precedence. Scorpio, however, rules the subconscious realm of transformation, regeneration, and the unseen. As above, so below. This astrological axis speaks about the fundamental themes of security and transformation, highlighting the interconnectedness between the physical and spiritual realms. It reminds us that stability comes not only in the form of material possessions but also from one’s inner strength and resilience.

Ready or not, this month’s intensity-filled full Pink Moon will peak on April 23 at 7:49 p.m. ET via four degrees of Scorpio. The full moon brings revelations, culminations, and full-circle moments. During this lunar phase, the sun makes a direct opposition with the moon, which serves as a call for balance between our physical reality (sun) and our inner world (moon). In this particular case, the sun in Taurus longs for practicality and simplicity, while the moon in Scorpio seeks truth beyond what is visible. 

While there’s no such thing as a mellow moon in Scorpio, April’s full moon is anything but. The sun and moon will square Pluto—Scorpio’s modern planetary ruler—which serves as the focal point in a challenging T-square. Also known as the Lord of the Underworld, Pluto is the planet of transformation, power, and everything that’s hidden beneath the surface. But it can also represent shadowy themes such as addiction, obsession, and the misuse of power. Pluto pushes us to confront our demons so that we can emerge stronger and more empowered.

It’s important to note that this is the first time we will experience such a hard aspect to Pluto in Aquarius, as it just recently entered this fixed air sign. This T-square is already pushing us outside our comfort zone, but the essence of Pluto in Aquarius is still unfamiliar territory. The growth we’re undergoing individually and collectively will likely revolve around Aquarian themes such as our associations, community, friendship groups, and sense of belonging in the world. This full moon revelation could be a tough pill to swallow, but it’s still a catalyst for our personal evolution. 

Don’t be afraid of the discomfort—you’re not alone. If anything, there is logic in your restlessness when considering the fact that the sun in Taurus resists change, the moon in Scorpio intensifies your emotions, and Pluto in Aquarius is showing you what you’ve outgrown. 

The less you resist, the better.
