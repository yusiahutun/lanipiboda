Will and Jada Smith's kids 'traumatized' and feel 'humiliated' by their marriage

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Will and Jada Smith’s marriage has been in the public eye ever since they tied the knot on New Year’s Eve 1997. Once known as couple goals across the globe, that may no longer be the case. After news started breaking about troubles in the marriage, including Jada’s ‘entanglement’ with August Alsina, it seems like the perception of the pair’s love story took a different direction.

Now, reports state the strained marriage and the private life of the celebrity couple being so public is having a negative effect. Insiders have claimed it’s ‘traumatizing’ kids Jaden and Willow, who allegedly feel ‘humiliated’ by Will and Jada Smith’s marriage.

Jaden and Willow Smith ‘acting out’ due to ‘Will and Jada’s strained marriage’

It has been reported Jaden, 25, and Willow, 22 are “extremely fragile” right now due to their parents’ behavior, sources tell the National Enquirer.

“Willow and Jaden are both extremely fragile right now and clearly traumatized by their parents’ behavior to the point where they’re acting out,” insiders say.

They added: “It seems to be having an adverse effect on their kids, too.”

“Willow is humiliated by the public way her parents have destroyed their marriage and each other,” the insider alleged. “She’s crying out for attention, causing fear for her well-being,” they claim.

Just recently, Jaden sparked concern with an Instagram photo. Fans could see paramedics in the next room during his trip to Milan, with medical bags strapped on their backs.

The post has since been deleted, however, his younger sister has since been sparking concern herself. The singer has been sharing cryptic quotes on her account, including one about her “condition worsening.”

The 22-year-old posted a picture of a cartoon character lying in a hospital bed and hooked up to a drip. It featured the text : “Every time I act nonchalant. My condition worsens.”

Insiders also claimed that the kids were “sending these twisted SOS calls for help.”

Celebrity Tidbit and GRV Media have approached Will Smith’s representatives for comment.

The couple have been separated for seven years

In a shocking revelation, Jada Pinkett Smith recently revealed the couple have actually been separated since 2016. The announcement came in an interview with Today, shortly before the release of her memoir, Worthy.

The Hollywood stars decided to “live completely separate lives” in 2016. Although it was not a “divorce on paper” it was a ‘divorce’. Therefore, Will and Jada Smith’s marriage has been the same for seven years.

This was unbeknownst to the public, as they had aired their troubles and “open marriage” but would still step out in public with a united front.

When asked by interviewer Hoda Kotb why they had never spoken out about this before now, the Red Table Talk host explained that they were just “not ready yet.”

“Still trying to figure out, between the two of us, how to be in partnership,” she said. “In regards to, how do we present that to people? We hadn’t figured that out.”

She said the relationship fractured for many reasons, “and by the time it got to 2016, we were just exhausted with trying.”
