Who is 'No Demo Reno' Star Jennifer Todryk's Husband?

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Fans of No Demo Reno know Jennifer Todryk's story and follow along as she transforms houses into beautiful homes. However, her husband Mike Todryk, who occasionally makes an appearance on the HGTV show, is a bit more of a mystery. Jenn (35), also known as the Rambling Redhead, and husband Mike (47) first met on August 6, 2010. At the time, Jenn was 23 and Mike was 35.

Exactly one year after meeting, the couple got married on August 6, 2011, in Mexico. Though they didn't date long before tying the knot, that hasn't seemed to be a problem for the Todryks. In 2023, the couple celebrated their 12th wedding anniversary. They have three children together: Von, Berkley, and Vivienne. "Mike is honestly the best girl dad," Jenn gushed on Instagram in February.

What does Mike Todryk do for a living?

No Demo Reno fans know how Jenn makes her fortune, but what about Mike? He attended West Point earning an engineering degree, serving in the Army after graduation. Later on in his career he went back to school, earning his MBA from Southern Methodist University. After retiring from the Army, Mike worked in Medical Device Sales for nearly 20 years, eventually leaving the industry in 2020, according to his LinkedIn profile.

Beginning in 2019, Mike started working toward his entrepreneurial goals. He began by opening Armor Coffee Co. in Allen, Texas. Along with the coffee shop, he is also working on his new venture: Armor Brewing Company, which is also located in Allen. The Todryks also co-host a podcast called You, Me & Mike, though they haven't released any new episodes this year. It seems Mike and Jenn have their hands full with everything they have going on, if only we got to see more of him on No Demo Reno.
