Black Friday might be in the rearview but today starts one of the biggest candle sales of the year. Bath & Body Works’ Candle Day 2023 features deep discounts on all of the brand’s 3-wick candles, including old favorites and new holiday scents. In fact, there are 40 Candle Day exclusive drops. Seriously!

If you’re a Bath & Body Works Loyalty Member, you get to shop first — December 1 in-store, in-app, and online. Becoming a rewards member is a no-brainer if you shop at Bath & Body Works more than a few times a year. It’s free to join. All new members get $10 off a $30 purchase. That’s an easy win. Otherwise, you’ll earn points with every purchase. $1 is equal to 10 points. If you spend $100, you earn 1,000 points and redeem a free full-size product (up to $16.50) of your choice. And you get early access to sales like Candle Day.

And you’ll want access. Because all 3-wick candles are just $9.95. Yes, all of them that are usually $26.50. There are more than 100 candles to choose from, with nearly 40 limited-edition candles dropping exclusively for the event. Everyone else can start shopping on December 2. The sale ends December 3.

And if you’re in NYC, head to Herald Square Plaza near the subway entrance on the corner of 34th Street & 6th Avenue to see (and smell) a Tis the Season 3-Wick Candle that’s literally 10 feet tall! It’ll only be on display November 30 and December 1.
