See All the Best Celebrity Costumes for Halloween 2022

April 2024 · 6 minute read

Halloween may fall on a Monday this year, but that just means there are more days to get into the spooky spirit. Our favorite celebs started the festivities early, kicking off the Halloweekend with several spotless renditions of jaw-dropping looks.

From Kylie Jenner posing in a couture gown as the Bride of Frankenstein, to Lizzo donning full yellow body paint as Marge Simpson, to Janelle Monáe transforming into a diva alien from The Fifth Element, 2022 has been the year for showing off major creativity and attention to detail.

See our favorite celebrity costumes below.

Kim Kardashian

The SKIMS founder went the superhero route, transforming into the X-Men character Mystique.

Kylie Jenner

The Kylie Cosmetics founder wore three stellar looks. She first paid homage to the 1935 film The Bride of Frankenstein in several looks, including a custom Jean Paul Gaultier gown by Glenn Martens.

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

For her second costume of the weekend, she was a sexy Elvira, Mistress of the Dark.

And for her third costume, the makeup mogul looked out of this world as a space girl.

Later on Monday, Jenner shared a sweet family photo on her Instagram Story of her, boyfriend Travis Scott, and their daughter, Stormi, wearing matching angel looks.

Kylie Jenner/Instagram

Hailey Bieber

The model channeled Laetitia Casta’s iconic rose wedding dress, which Casta wore at Yves Saint Laurent's haute couture runway show in 1999.

Later, she transformed into a Versace vampire, wearing a sultry all-black ensemble—a bra top, miniskirt, opera gloves, garter, and gold chain belt spelling out the Italian brand's name. She was spotted out with husband Justin Bieber, who embodied the Cookie Monster in a cozy blue onesie.

Bella Hadid

The supermodel has been booked and busy in Qatar this Halloween, so she took the symbolic route as a "walking heart" for the holiday, looking stunning in a red gown, courtesy of Italian designer Schiaparelli.

Heidi Klum

The queen of Halloween is back with another bizarre, over-the-top look. This year for her costume, the model dressed up as a slimy pink worm, and husband Tom Kaulitz as the creepy fisherman who caught her.

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

She attended her annual lavish Halloween bash—Heidi Klum's Hallowe'en Party at Sake No Hana—in the fit, and later stripped off her worm attire (but kept the face), revealing a sultry silver and sheer lingerie jumpsuit.

Leni Klum

Heidi Klum's teen daughter, Leni Klum, went the more traditional route with her costume, dressing up as Catwoman in full leather catsuit, mask with ears, and heeled boots. At the party, she posed on the carpet for a hilarious photo with her mom.

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

Janelle Monáe

The singer and actor pulled off a stunning full-body transformation into Diva Plavalaguna, the alien singer from The Fifth Element.

Lizzo

The "About Damn Time" singer transformed into Marge Simpson, complete with full body paint and matching yellow nails.

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelley

The couple dressed up as Baywatch actress Pamela Anderson and Mötley Crüe drummer Tommy Lee, specifically the couple's looks from the 1995 grand opening party for the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino in Las Vegas.

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker

Mr. and Mrs. Barker dressed up as Chucky and the Bride of Chucky for their first Halloween as a married couple.

The two attended their family Halloween bash with Kourtney's sisters and their kids.

Khloé Kardashian/Instagram

Lori Harvey

The model paid homage to Beyoncé, re-creating the superstar's looks from her 2003 "Me, Myself, and I" music video.

Ariana Grande and Liz Gillies

The former Victorious stars re-created several scenes from the 2000 cult-classic mockumentary Best in Show (including Grande's latest Jennifer Coolidge impersonation).

Frankie Jonas and Anna Olsen

The youngest Jonas and his girlfriend poked fun at Joe Jonas and ex Taylor Swift by hilariously re-creating one of the former couple's most iconic stage moments.

Joe Jonas

Joe, meanwhile, went all out as Flo from Progressive—bright red lipstick and all.

Ciara

The singer and her five-year-old, Sienna, re-created Serena and Venus Williams's 2000 "Got Milk?" campaign, complete with white milk mustaches.

Paris Hilton

The reality star first dressed as her "childhood icon," Sailor Moon. Later, she channeled a sultry FBI agent via an all-black look consisting of a cropped jacket, mini skirt, and thigh-high heeled boots.

Halle Bailey

The singer lived out her dreams of becoming an Avatar character with a convincing head-to-toe blue humanoid look coordinated alongside that of artist boyfriend DDG.

Chlöe Bailey

The "For the Night" singer made an electrifying Storm.

Keke Palmer

Weeks after she responded to fans who wanted her to play Rapunzel, the Nope actress made a stunning princess in extra-long box braids.

Kendall Jenner

The model tapped into '90s nostalgia as Jessie from Pixar's Toy Story 2. And on Monday, she made good humor out of her viral cucumber cutting moment from Hulu’s The Kardashians with a legendary cucumber slice costume poking fun at that scene.

Lili Reinhart, Madelaine Petsch, and Camila Mendes

The Riverdale trio dressed in updated looks based on Bette Midler, Sarah Jessica Parker, and Kathy Najimy's witches from Hocus Pocus.

Latto

The "Big Energy" rapper transformed into Emily from Tim Burton's Corpse Bride with the help of a stunning eye look.

Hayley Kiyoko and Becca Tilley

The singer and the Bachelor Nation alum made an adorable pair in their couple's costume, with Kiyoko as Velma and Tilley as Daphne from Scooby-Doo.

Eugene Lee Yang

The filmmaker and Try Guy pulled double duty as Eddie Munson and Vecna from Stranger Things Season 4.

Addison Rae

The TikTok star re-created Lady Gaga's performance look from the 2009 VMAs, fake blood and all.

Sarah Michelle Gellar

The Do Revenge star traded in her headmaster power suits for the Netflix film's adorable school uniform.

Vanessa Hudgens

The multi-hyphenate planned a two-person Black Swan costume, with her dancing the White Swan and friend GG Magree dancing as the Black Swan.

MUNA

The electro-pop band turned their concert into a Halloween party, dressing as the band Pink Slip from 2003's Freaky Friday to perform the soundtrack song "Take Me Away."

Jordyn Woods

Woods took after Diana Ross with her famous sparkling fishnet catsuit and pose from the singer's iconic 1969 cover photoshoot by legendary photographer Richard Avedon.

Vanessa Bryant

Bryant coordinated an adorable family costume with her little ones, dressing as a mother lion to her two cubs.

Nicola and Brooklyn Peltz Beckham

The Peltz Beckhams channeled Shakespeare's Romeo & Juliet. The actress wore an ethereal white gown with angel wings, and her model husband looked knightly in a black armored vest alongside a sword.

Kerry Washington

Washington embodied singer Lionel Richie from head to toe, alongside an uncanny reenactment of the poster art from his famous vinyl LP.
