Early Life and Background

Daniel Kaluuya was born on February 24, 1989, in London, England. He comes from Ugandan heritage, with his parents immigrating to the UK in the 1980s.

Childhood Passion for Acting

From a young age, Kaluuya showed a flair for the dramatic. He participated in school plays and local theater productions, nurturing his love for acting.

Breakthrough Performance in “Skins”

One of Kaluuya’s first major roles was in the hit British TV series “Skins.” His portrayal of Posh Kenneth gained him widespread recognition and praise.

Collaborations with Renowned Filmmakers

Kaluuya has had the privilege of working with acclaimed directors such as Jordan Peele, Steve McQueen, and Ryan Coogler, enhancing his reputation as a versatile actor.

Memorable Role in “Get Out”

Kaluuya’s performance as Chris Washington in Jordan Peele’s “Get Out” garnered critical acclaim, earning him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.
