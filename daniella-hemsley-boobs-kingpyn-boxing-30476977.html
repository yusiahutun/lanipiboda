And it was England's Hemsley who came up trumps on the evening, edging a decision to mark her first win in the ring. The rise of the influencer boxing scene has made weigh-in nudity something of a common sight in boxing, but the post-scrap strip is still a novelty.

It may catch on with the Instagram and TikTok star pioneering the way, however, after she pulled up her bra and screamed with joy following a titillating display.
