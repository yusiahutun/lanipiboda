Cheban formerly had a girlfriend named Anat Popovsky, which smacked down all the speculations of him being gay. Anat and Cheban began dating in the year 2013 and had made various public appearances with each other.

According to the source, things were going smoothly, and they were getting so serious that even Kim was okay with Anat dating her BFF. The source stated-
