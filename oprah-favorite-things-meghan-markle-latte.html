Meghan Markle loves this health-boosting coffee - and it's her friend Oprah's favorite, too

March 2024 · 3 minute read

The Duchess of Sussex's favorite health-boosting coffee Clevr Blends is a staple on Oprah's Favorite Things list

    Meghan Markle's favorite health-boosting coffee is also loved by her good friend, and Montecito neighbor, Oprah.

    The superfood-packed oat milk lattes by Clevr Blends have both celebrity and royal approval after landing on Oprah's Favorite Things list two years in a row.

    "This woman-led wellness brand is beloved by my neighbor Meghan—and I’m a big fan, too!" Oprah said.
