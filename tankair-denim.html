Tank Air's New Denim Collection Is Full of 'It Girl' Must-Haves

March 2024 · 4 minute read

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

Tank Air, known for their viral Studio Tanks worn by Bella Hadid, is venturing into the denim world with their newest collection. The brand announced on Instagram that their denim capsule consisting of three pieces, will drop on their website today. 

Established in 2019 by Claire Robertson-Macleod, Tank Air describes themselves as “in service of the Matriarchy.” Made in LA, Tank Air references Robertson-Macleod’s Thai roots through their many viral designs. The brand has quickly established themselves as the It Girl brand after being worn by Bella Hadid, Olivia Rodrigo, Emily Ratajkowski, and Lisa from Blackpink, among others. 

Although their viral $70 Studio Tanks have been criticized online for their price, the brand is known for making high quality pieces. In November of last year, Tank Air had their first ever sale, answering the prayers of their fanbase. While their prices are a bit short of affordable– every piece I personally own from Tank Air was constructed beautifully and holds up better than anything I own from fast fashion retailers.

After a year and a half in the making, Tank Air’s first denim capsule consists of three pieces: the Low Slung Jeans, Denim Mini Skirt, and Cocoon Denim Maxi Skirt. The pieces are constructed with 12.5-13 oz denim and finished with custom engraved brass-tone hardware. 

If you’re not on the hunt for new denim don’t fret, the brand has tons of It Girl essentials that you’ve probably already seen your favorite celebrities wearing. I’ve made a list of all the new denim pieces available to shop now as well as some of my personal favorites from the brand.

  • Low Slung Jeans

    Tank Air Low Slung Jeans

    Tank Air’s new Low Slung Jeans are low waisted straight leg jeans– straight out of a Bella Hadid inspired Pinterest board. The jeans come in a unique limestone color and are subtly faded and distressed throughout. They come in sizes XXS-XXL, but run big on purpose so they can sit low on the waist.

  • Denim Mini Skirt

    Tank Air Denim Mini Skirt

    A denim mini skirt is a staple in every It Girl wardrobe. It comes in a faded black color and is the perfect length for running around New York City in the summer. Olivia Rodrigo loves a good mini skirt, so we’re sure to see her in this soon.

  • Cocoon Denim Maxi Skirt

    Tank Air Cocoon Denim Maxi Skirt

    For all the maxi skirt fans, the Cocoon Denim Maxi Skirt. This skirt is a beautiful indigo color and has a slit in the back. Denim maxi skirts are back and not in a kindergarten art teacher way, they’re for the It Girls now!

  • Studio Tank

    Tank Air Studio Tank

    The Studio Tank was on everyone’s radar last summer after Bella Hadid was seen wearing it. Although it received a fair share of criticism, it’s a great quality basic tank. It comes in 5 versatile colors and will definitely be making the rounds again when it gets warmer.

  • Compassionate Baby Tee

    Tank Air Compassionate Baby Tee

    Seen on both Bella Hadid and Olivia Rodrigo, the Compassionate Baby Tee is one of Tank Air’s most iconic pieces. Its black and white colorway makes it perfect for everyday wear and it can be dressed up or down.

  • Tank Air Bunny Knit

    Tank Air Bunny Knit

    My personal favorite, the Tank Air Bunny Knit. The year of the rabbit has come and gone, but my Bunny Knit remains a staple in my wardrobe. With the “Tank Air” logo in Thai at the chest, the Bunny Knit goes with everything. It comes in black, sakura (a rosy baby pink,) and olive grey. 

  • Hot Mesh Top

  • Lotus Mini Skirt

    Tank Air Lotus Mini Skirt

    Emily Ratajkowski paired the Lotus Mini Skirt with her Hot Mesh Top. It comes in 5 colors and is adorned with lotus flowers across the hips. Also made of silk and cashmere, it’s super soft and comfortable to wear.
