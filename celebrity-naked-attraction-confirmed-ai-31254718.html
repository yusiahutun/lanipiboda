However, throughout the years, rumours have been rife that a celebrity edition of the series could be made. And now, it’s been claimed that the celeb version is going ahead. What’s more, host Anna has even revealed the celebs who told her they’d be open for an appearance on the outrageous dating show.

Daily Star has quizzed one of the world’s most popular artificially intelligent “creative” tools Bard AI, to see if a Celebrity Naked Attraction could be made. The program responded: “There is always the possibility of a celebrity naked attraction in the future.”

Bard AI explained: “Particularly if the show's producers are able to attract a high-profile celebrity to take part. Additionally, if the show's format is changed or updated in a way that makes it more appealing to celebrities, it is possible that we could see a celebrity naked attraction in the future.”

What’s more, in June, host Anna revealed the names of the celebs willing to strip naked for a celebrity special. “I’ve bumped into a couple of celebs recently who’ve gone ‘I’d do it!’,” Anna told OK! magazine.

She went on: “Duncan James was like, ‘I’m up for it!’, and I was like ‘oh my god!’. And the lovely Anna Whitehouse, who does the Dirty Mother Pukka podcast, said to me a couple of weeks ago that she’d do it.”
