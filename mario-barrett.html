Mario Barrett’s Career

The R&B singer Mario Barrett started his singing career in his mid-teens. Initially, he was signed to the New York label 3rd Street/J Records and worked with producer Clive Davis. During his career at J Records, he contributed to the soundtrack of Dr. Dolittle 2 (2001).
