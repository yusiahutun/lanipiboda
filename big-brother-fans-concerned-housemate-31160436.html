Big Brother fans concerned for housemate as they go 'missing' just days into show

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Big Brother viewers were left stumped after housemate Paul Blackburn seemed to have disappeared just days into the reboot airing.

The iconic series made its epic return to ITV screens on Sunday evening (October 8). Fronted by AJ Odudu and Will Best, the programme welcomed a brand new batch of housemates moving into the famous house.

And one of those is Paul, a security officer from Liverpool. However, a few episodes into the new series, and fans have shared their fears for his whereabouts.
