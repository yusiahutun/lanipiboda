Declan Donnelly fell for wife as she worked as Ant and Dec manager

March 2024 · 4 minute read

Declan Donnelly, 42, took to Twitter to confirm the happy news that he and his wife, Ali, 40, are having a baby after reports started to surface.

The Saturday Night Takeaway presenter shared a black and wife photo from the pair’s wedding, followed by the message: “Just wanted to say thank you for all the lovely messages.

“The news has sneaked out a little earlier than we had hoped but Ali and I are delighted to be expecting our first child.

“Thanks for the love, we really appreciate it.”

The news comes after an emotional week for Dec, after his best friend Ant McPartlin was arrested on suspicion of drink driving and returned to rehab.

Ali was born in Newcastle, Australia in 1978.

She was raised by her mother Angela, a retired air stewardess, and father Anthony, a retired purser, in a village outside Woking.

She also has has a brother, Simon, who is the creative director at a music company.

After college she worked at a building company, before starting to work for James Grant Management in 2003.

Ali works as Ant & Dec’s manager through James Grant Management in London.

She also deals with various other famous faces, including Fearne Cotton, Tess Daly, Marvin and Rochelle Humes, and Holly Willoughby.

Her mother told the Daily Mail: “She’s very organised. Soon she was managing Anthea Turner and Philip Schofield – and Ant and Dec.

“When she told me, I thought she said she was managing someone called Anton Dec.

“Ali works very hard, it’s a 24-hour job and very stressful sometimes, although she loves her work.”

The happy couple became engaged on November 12, 2014 after dating for a year.

However, they had known each other for over a decade and were good friends before they started seeing each other.

Dec admitted when they got engaged that there was always something between him and his manager: “I think we’ve always been attracted to each other but it has always been that thing of we work together.

“This is the first time we’ve given it a try. We always hung out.

“When she was single and I was single we’d go out for dinner on Valentine’s night together because we didn’t have partners.

“This year we went out as a couple.”

Ali used to live 200 metres from Dec’s eight-bedroom house in Chiswick, West London, before moving in together in 2014.

They said their vows at St Michael’s Church in Elswick, Tyne and Wear on August 1, 2015.

His co-host and best pal Ant McPartlin was his best man, and 200 guests watched them tie the knot.

Joker Ant pulled a prank on his friend at the wedding, swapping their first dance song to their PJ & Duncan hit Let’s Get Ready To Rhumble.

Dec has been linked to various celebs, including former Pussycat Doll and I’m A Celebrity… Get Me Out Of Here! contestant Ashley Roberts in 2013.

He also dated Sky Sports presenter Georgie Thompson for two years before splitting in 2011.

Before that Dec had been in a longterm relationship with his childhood sweetheart and actress Clare Buckfield for 11 years.

Clare became a household name after shooting to fame with the children’s show Grange Hill and sitcom Two Point Four Children.

The pair split in 2003.

It is believed that the parents-to-be got pregnant shortly after returning from filming the last  series of I’m A Celebrity in December last year.

According to reports, they wanted to keep it secret until Ali had passed the 12-week mark.

A source told The Sun  that the happy couple had broken the news to close friends and family, including Ant when that period was over.

The baby will most likely be born in the autumn.

Last year Dec revealed that he “absolutely” wanted to have children.

He said: “Absolutely at some point, when the time is right. Hopefully, as Ant says, if it comes along, then it would be great.”
