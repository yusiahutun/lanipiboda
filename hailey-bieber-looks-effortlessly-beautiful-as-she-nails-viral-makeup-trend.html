Hailey Bieber looks 'effortlessly beautiful' as she nails viral makeup trend

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Hailey Bieber is just like us as she (very successfully) attempts the viral latte makeup trend that has been doing the rounds on TikTok. However, in true Bieber style, Hailey took the trend one step further as she incorporated her outfit into the look, and she absolutely slayed.

The 26-year-old model is no stranger to serving looks on the runway and online. We take a look at Hailey Bieber‘s ‘gorgeous’ latte makeup, and how fans were reacting in the comments.

Hailey Bieber takes part in viral latte makeup trend

Taking to her (almost) 50 million Instagram followers, Hailey posted a selfie of herself. It follows the viral Tiktok makeup trend, latte makeup.

For those who may be unaware, the latte makeup trend is all about using warm neutrals with a caramel tone.

People following the trend usually consist of tanned based with a warm smoky eye, rounded off with a nude lip. Of course, Bieber absolutely slays.

The 26-year-old took things one step further though, and as her caption says, took the trend “head to toe.” She stunned in a gold cut-out dress, pairing it with matching bags and earrings to complete the ‘latte look.’

Hailey also gave fans a rundown of her whole routine over on TikTok, using her skincare brand, Rhode Skin.

Fans say the model looks ‘effortlessly beautiful’

As soon as the photo graced fans timelines, they rushed to the comments to compliment the celebrity model on her ‘viral look.’

“She’s so effortlessly beautiful I’m crying,” wrote one fan.

Bestie and Hollywood star Kylie Jenner simply wrote: “gorgeous.”

“No filter we love a natural queen,” penned another.

One stunned fan commented: “Let’s talk about how good beige looks on you. Wow.”

Hailey flaunts $500k engagement ring ending ‘split’ speculation

The viral latte makeup look comes days after Hailey showed off her $500k engagement ring amid rumors she and husband Justin may have ‘split.’

However, there were no facts to back up the rumor, just the mere fact that Hailey didn’t include her husband in her latest photo dump.

All speculation has now been cleared with the sighting of the rock, and Hailey’s cute pic with Justin Bieber and friends via her Instagram story since.
