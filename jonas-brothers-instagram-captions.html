Lyrics To Share To Social Media

May 2024 · 6 minute read

With over 90 shows, over 60 songs, and a performance of a lifetime, these Jonas Brothers Instagram captions will make your friends *Nick Jonas voice* so jealous!

The JoBros stunned a sold-out opening weekend at Yankees Stadium in New York City after a sold-out Broadway run that also showcased their five albums 2007’s Jonas Brothers; 2008’s A Little Longer; 2009’s Lines, Vines and Trying Times; 2019’s Happiness Begins; and their upcoming fifth album, The Album. As you can tell, performing over 90% of your discography is a behemoth task to accomplish, but the Jonas Brothers did it with ease. The show even sprinkled some fun surprises like Big Rob making a cameo during “Burnin’ Up.” Safe to say, every Jonas Brothers stan will be satisfied with this incredible show.

“I spent literally three, four months building this thing out with our team, song by song, where we could tell the story we wanted to tell about the journey we were on at that point in our life and career,” Nick Jonas recalled about forming the setlist to Today. Well, it all worked out when he said this during their opening night at the world-famous stadium: “I’m so proud we got (ourselves) together so we could be here tonight. We’ve seen a lot of people who have been with us from the beginning.”

So what are you waiting for and don’t wait “A Little Bit Longer”, use these Jonas Brothers Instagram captions now!
