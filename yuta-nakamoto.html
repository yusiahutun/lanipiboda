Yuta Nakamoto Biography

July 2024 · 2 minute read

It was rumored that he moved to South Korea to complete his high school education. He is a singer and rapper who is the only Japanese member of the K-pop boy band NCT. His position on the side is a vocalist, rapper, and lead dancer. They made their debut with singles "The 7th Sense", and "Without You." He started his career, as a trainee on S.M. Entertainment. During his stint as S.M.Entertainment trainee, he appeared regularly in the talk show Non-Summit.

In March 2016, he became the selected model for the British Casual clothing band “Design United" along with other two trainees from S.M. Entertainment. In the same year, S.M. Entertainment announced his creation of NCT, a k-pop group that would become active all over the world along with its sub-units. He was expected to be a member of NCT. Currently, he is a member of the group's second sub-unit NCT#127.

Yuta Nakamoto was a part of NCT#127, which was placed at 32 on the in their debut year 2016, which sold over 83,272 copies. The album peaked 22nd position and 2 in US Billboard Heatseekers Album and US Billboards World albums respectively.

Yuta is quite confidential about his personal life; he was rumored several times for having a girlfriend or for having an affair; though, it is yet to confirm. And he has even been spotted hanging around with a girl in public places.

His net worth is unknown as he hasn't revealed his salary yet; however, according to sources, his favorite accessory is an earring. His birthplace is Osaka Japan, where he resides parently along with his two sisters.
