About

Pauly Shore is a 56 year old American Comedian. Born Paul Montgomery Shore on 1st February, 1968 in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California, USA, he is famous for Encino Man in a career that spans 1985–present. His zodiac sign is Aquarius.

Kylie Minogue is a 55 year old Australian Singer. Born Kylie Ann Minogue on 28th May, 1968 in Bethlehem Hospital, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, she is famous for her roles as Charlene Robinson in the long-running Seven Network/Network Ten/10 Peach soap opera Neighbours, Lola Lovell in the 1989 coming-of-age romantic drama film The Delinquents, Cammy in the 1994 action film Street Fighter, the Green Fairy in the 2001 jukebox musical romantic drama film Moulin Rouge!, Eva Grace in the 2012 surrealist fantasy drama film Holy Motors, Susan Riddick in the 2015 disaster film San Andreas, and Kaye Hall in the 2018 comedy-drama film Swinging Safari, in addition to her hit singles, including The Loco-Motion, Especially for You, Better the Devil You Know, Can’t Get You Out of My Head, Confide in Me, Spinning Around, Slow, 2 Hearts, and All the Lovers in a career that spans 1987–present. Her zodiac sign is Gemini.
