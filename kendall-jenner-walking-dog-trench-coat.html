Kendall Jenner Executes a Lesson in Glamour on a Dog Walk

February 2024 · 2 minute read

It’s always an accomplishment if you manage to put on more than a hoodie and sweatpants for the long and winding journey outside to walk your dog. But for celebrities, a casual stroll with the pooch on a well-lit sidewalk is also the perfect opportunity to show off their style.

For instance, Jennifer Lawrence went viral earlier this year for the elegant ensemble she wore while walking a pack of three dogs, a stunt she pulled off for a Longines ad. And who could forget the many, many, many paparazzi pics of Emily Ratajkowski walking her husky/German shepherd mix, Colombo, in New York City? Now, Kendall Jenner has entered the chat.

Bottega Veneta Multicolor Wool Twill Trench

Bottega Veneta Multicolor Wool Twill Trench

Yesterday afternoon, the model was spotted on a Beverly Hills promenade, accompanied by her Doberman pinscher, Pyro. She looked extremely glamorous for the casual outing, continuing her Bottega Veneta love affair in a sophisticated belted taupe trench coat, a maroon wool sleeveless top with a patterned collar, dark-cherry heeled leather boots, and black-rimmed aviator sunglasses with warm yellow lenses.

BACKGRID

This isn’t the first time Jenner has made mundane tasks look like a fashion show.

Just last month, the 818 founder was photographed at a gas station in yet another jaw-dropping Bottega Veneta outfit. Then, she wore a luxe navy blue collared dress with white trimming, a belted waist, and a voluminous tiered skirt. For a pop of color, she added bright yellow and neon-green striped iteration of the house’s woven Canalazzo boots.
