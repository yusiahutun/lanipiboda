See Emily Ratajkowski's New Tattoo of Her Son's Name

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski is showing off her love for her baby boy with some forever ink.

On Instagram, the model and My Body author recently shared photos of a new tattoo dedicated to her son, Sylvester Apollo Bear, whom she shares with husband Sebastian Bear-McClard.

The picture captures the side of a foot and the inside of two arms, all of which are inked with "Sly," her one-year-old's nickname, in a minimalist font. A second photo shows baby Sly sitting on Ratajkowski's lap while the duo are seated at a restaurant.

She captioned the photos, "Sly forever ♾."

Since Ratajkowski and Bear-McClard welcomed the arrival of their first child in March 2021, the model has shared multiple photos of Sly via social media. She's also spoken out about her own personal development as a mother.

In a joint interview with author Lisa Taddeo for ELLE last November, she opened up about her complicated feelings about discovering her baby's sex while she was pregnant.

"I wanted a daughter initially, but when I found out I was having a son, I was so relieved," she said. "I want more children, so it might be something I deal with later—being sexualized way before puberty and being aware of it. I have a memory: I did a sexy move down the wall of my parents' kitchen. I was probably in first grade and my parents were like, 'Where did you learn that?' I was like, 'I fricking learned it. That's what women do.'"
