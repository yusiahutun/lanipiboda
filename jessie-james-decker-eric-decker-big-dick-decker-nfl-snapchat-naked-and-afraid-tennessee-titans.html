Jessie James Decker thinks "we've got serious problems" if people were upset with her for calling her NFL star husband "big dick decker" on social media while celebrating a touchdown.

While speaking to TooFab Wednesday about the Season 3 premiere of her E! reality show "Eric and Jessie," the country singer married to Tennessee Titans wide receiver Eric Decker reflected on the media's reaction to the Snapchat she sent out after he caught a 5-yard touchdown pass last season against the Buffalo Bills.

"I came up with it because I always called him that to be funny," Decker told TooFab. "I swear when I posted that I didn't think it was going to get attention like it did. When it did, I was like, 'Wait, what?' I didn't think it was that big of a deal."

The country music artist is aware she often says "crazy" things, but that's just one of the many things Eric loves about her.
