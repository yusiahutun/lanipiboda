Bon Jovi To Curate Two Concert Cruises In 2019

August 2024 · 2 minute read

MIAMI (CelebrityAccess) — Jon Bon Jovi announced that he has signed on to curate a pair of high seas concert cruises, which are slated to sail in 2019.

Fans can select either a Carribean cruise, which will set sail April 12-16, 2019 from Miami to Nassau, Bahamas, on the cruise ship Norwegian Jade, or a Mediterranean cruise which departs August 26-30, 2019, from Barcelona to Palma, Majorca, on the Norwegian Pearl.

The cruises, which are co-produced by Runaway Tours in partnership with Sixthman, will feature music, including a full rock show on the pool deck starring Jon Bon Jovi and the 11-piece Kings of Suburbia band, and a second acoustic storyteller set where Bon Jovi will participate in a Q&A session.

The cruises will also feature multiple shows from (TBA) support artists, as well as panels with key figures from Bon Jovi’s past.

“Ever since we started Runaway Tours, it’s given me such joy to entertain and tell stories of where my songs came from. What I’ve realized after all these years is that my fans have found that these are not only my stories, but their own,” says Jon Bon Jovi “Hundreds of people have gotten together every time we take off for a destination, and long-lasting friendships are born. We want to continue that tradition in Runaway’s fully-immersive vacation trips.”

Double occupancy staterooms on Runaway To Paradise begin at $1,895 (USD) per person for the Caribbean sailing, and $2,221 (USD) per person for the Mediterranean sailing, plus taxes and fees. Prices for the four-night music festivals at sea include access to all shows and activities, all meals and non-alcoholic beverages, as well as a variety of swag such as a customized Jon Bon Jovi Runaway to Paradise gift, commemorative event program, custom event lanyard, and laminate.
