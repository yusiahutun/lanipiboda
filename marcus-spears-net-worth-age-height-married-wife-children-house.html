Who Is Marcus Raishon Spears? Bio & Wiki

Marcus Spears was born as Marcus Raishon Spears on 8th March 1983, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, the United States. Moreover, he holds an American nationality and his ethnicity is African-American.

Spears attended Southern University Laboratory School where he became the No. 1 tight end. He made 28 catches for 435 yards and 3 touchdowns and 245 rushing yards, 22 tackles, 11.5 quarterback sacks on defense.

Later, Spears graduated from the Louisiana State University where he was trained by a football coach, Nick Saban. HE further played for the LSU Tigers football team from 2001 to 2004.  Later, Spears finished college career with 49 tackles and 21 quarterback pressures in total defense.

Inside Of Marcus Spears’ Career

Marcus Spears started his career as an NFL Draft player after being picked by the Dallas Cowboys with an overall 20 picks in 2005. Later, he made debut at the San Diego but didn’t record a tackle. Spears recorded his first tackle against Greg Elis of Washington Redskins. He played for Dallas Cowboys for overall 8 years from 2005 to 2013.

After playing for many years for Dallas Cowboys, Spears signed a two-year with the Baltimore Ravens on 15th March 2013. Later, he retired from the football ground with 10 tackles as a reserve defensive end on 30th October 2013. Spears made his debut appearance as a host on the TV show, DFW Outdoorsman in 2015. He also worked for many TV shows including SEC Nation, Thinking Out Loud along with Greg McElroy.
