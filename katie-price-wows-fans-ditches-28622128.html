Katie Price wows fans as she ditches makeup in filter free snap after Carl Woods split

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Katie Price was praised by relieved fans who enjoyed seeing a post from the star "with no filters".

The glamour model posted a video of her getting her makeup done for a shoot, adding the caption: "So much fun shooting today... I can't wait for you all to see the pics".

Fans were quick to notice the video had no filters applied to it, a hack that Katie regularly uses across her feed.
