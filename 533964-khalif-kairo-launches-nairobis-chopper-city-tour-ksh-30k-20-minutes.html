Khalif Kairo Launches Nairobi's First Chopper City Tour at KSh 30k for 20 Minutes

September 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Khalif Kairo, a renowned entrepreneur in the car import business expanded his portfolio to include planes
  • The shrewd businessman sells aircraft, offers empty leg flights and recently announced aerial tours of Nairobi
  • A section of social media users said the price was too steep, but most people lauded his business acumen

Pilot and car enthusiast Khalif Kairo has launched a helicopter sightseeing tour over Nairobi City.

How much does Nairobi helicopter tour cost?

A poster seen by Tuko.co.ke showed that Kairo's tour gives tourists a bird's eye flight around Nairobi at a cost of KSh 30,000 per person.

The 20-minute trip will begin at Wilson Airport, South of Nairobi.

Tourists fly over Kibra, Waiyaki Way, then Kiambu Road, before heading towards town via Thika Road and landing back at Wilson.

While the reaction to the launch was mixed, many lauded the entrepreneur for providing affordable and accessible helicopter tours.

"You need to introduce the same in Mombasa. Aerial views of the coastline can be breathtaking."

Patrick Mwangi:
