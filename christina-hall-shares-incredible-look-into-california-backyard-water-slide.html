Christina Hall shares incredible look into California backyard with gigantic water slide

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Christina on the Coast star also has a home in Nashville

    Christina Hall has given fans a glimpse into her incredible Newport Beach backyard after purchasing a Happy Hop Shark Cave Adventure for her three children. The HGTV presenter took to Instagram to reveal to fans that she had visited her local Costco for pet food but was walking out the store with much more - including the water slide! 

    The "exciting and entertaining inflatable water slide" claims to "inflate in minutes," and features a slide that runs through the inside of the shark, and exits the rider into a pool of cool water. There is also a ladder and a water gun to aim at unsuspecting riders. The slide retails for $419.99. 

    In Christina's video her two sons Hudson, three, and Brayden, seven, could be seen in their swim shorts as the slide began to fill with water. 

    The home, in Newport Beach, California was purchased by Christina and her husband Josh Hall in 2022.

    "Celebrating and settling in," she captioned her Instagram post in June 2022, as she shared how they " made a wish list of every single thing we wanted in a home and then the next day this is off market". 

    Christina is mom to three kids; Hudson with her ex, Ant Anstead, and Brayden and daughter Taylor, 12, with her first ex-husband, Tarek El Moussa. She married Josh Hall in 2022.

    They moved from Dana Point back to Newport Beach last tyar to settle into a "long-term family home". 

    "We moved to Dana Point last summer after falling in love with the ocean view,” the 39-year-old wrote on Instagram in April 2022. 

    "We still LOVE the view, but among other things, we don’t love the 90-minute round trip drive to and from the kids’ school. Back to Newport we go. Near school, friends and work.” 

    The family also has a beautiful Nashville farmhouse, which she purchased in late 2021. 

    Christina is currently embarking on an exciting new chapter in her life after her long-running show with first husband, Tarek came to an end in September 2022. She now stars in and but will next be seen in HGTV"s Barbie Dreamhouse Challenge.
