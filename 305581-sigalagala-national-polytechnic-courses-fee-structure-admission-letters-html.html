Sigalagala National Polytechnic courses, fee structure, and admission letters

February 2024 Â· 5 minute read

Sigalagala National Polytechnic is your go-to training institution if you want to gain hands-on experience and horn your entrepreneur skills. The institution's motto is to adequately train its students without compromising on the quality of education. That approach of teaching has enabled this polytechnic to continue serving students from different parts of Kenya over many decades.

Sigalagala Poly started in 1950 and has grown from a trade school to a National Polytechnic that currently boasts of an enrolment of over 4000 trainees. In turn, it produces an almost equivalent number of graduates.

The underlying goal of this institution is to impart skills, transform lives and growing the economy. That goal is possible because it prides itself in producing not only a highly skilled graduate but also responsible, competent individuals in society.

Sigalagala Technical Training Institute courses minimum requirements

For certificate courses, one is required to have attained a minimum of D (plain) in KCSE

For diploma courses, one is required to have attained a minimum of C- (minus) in KCSE

Sigalagala fees structure

The fees depend on the course one has been admitted to take. The fee structure is usually attached to the admission letter.

Sigalagala National Polytechnic admission letter

Anyone interested in joining Sigalagala Poly is first allowed to choose a course of their liking. If one meets the minimum requirement for the desired course, they are given an admission letter officially inviting them to join the school. In the admission letter, all the admission requirements are listed, depending on the course.
