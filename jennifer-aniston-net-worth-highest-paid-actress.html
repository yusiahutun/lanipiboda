Keep scrolling to find out everything you need to know about the A-lister's jaw-dropping net worth…

    Jennifer Aniston's net worth

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Jennifer is worth around an incredible $320 million, earning upwards of $30 million per year from not only her acting roles, but also her advertising endorsements, which include: SmartWater, Aveeno skincare, Vital Proteins, and Emirates airline - a deal that reportedly cost $5 million.

    Jennifer's record-breaking salary has previously appeared in the Forbes' list for highest-earning actresses since 2006, and was the second-highest on the list in 2017 after Emma Stone, earning more than Jennifer Lawrence, Emma Watson and Mila Kunis.

    Jennifer Aniston's haircare brand

    Given the cult status of Rachel Green's sleek hair, it may come as no surprise to learn that Jennifer has her very own haircare brand: LolaVie. The award-winning range offers an array of cruelty-free and vegan products made from naturally-derived ingredients - and without parabens, silicones, sulfates, phthalates, and gluten.

    The US-based brand is thought to have added an annual $15-20 million to Jennifer's jaw-dropping net worth.
