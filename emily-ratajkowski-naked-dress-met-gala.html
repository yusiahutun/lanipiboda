Emily Ratajkowski gave us the most daring look of the night on the 2019 Met Gala red carpet.

In her interpretation of this year's theme, 'Camp: Notes on Fashion', the model-actress arrived in the ultimate naked dress. Her custom Dundas design, which sparkled from head-to-toe, featured a full bodice cut-out, open back and a halter-neck tie.

She accessorised with an elaborate headpiece that featured feathered wings, a diamond headband and strands of jewels that draped from ear-to-ear.

Many fans were quick to point out the similarities between Ratajkowski's daring look and Cher's signature naked-dress style from over the years, courtesy of designer Bob Mackie.
