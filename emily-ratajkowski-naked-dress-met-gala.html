Emily Ratajkowski paid tribute to Cher's original Met Gala naked dress

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski gave us the most daring look of the night on the 2019 Met Gala red carpet.

In her interpretation of this year's theme, 'Camp: Notes on Fashion', the model-actress arrived in the ultimate naked dress. Her custom Dundas design, which sparkled from head-to-toe, featured a full bodice cut-out, open back and a halter-neck tie.

Theo Wargo//Getty Images

Gilbert Carrasquillo//Getty Images

She accessorised with an elaborate headpiece that featured feathered wings, a diamond headband and strands of jewels that draped from ear-to-ear.

Many fans were quick to point out the similarities between Ratajkowski's daring look and Cher's signature naked-dress style from over the years, courtesy of designer Bob Mackie.

Getty Images

Ron Galella//Getty Images

Mackie has been behind some of Cher's most iconic 'naked' looks. He's been a huge pioneering figure in the history of camp fashion, so it's little surprise that he was asked to serve on the board of the Met Gala this year.

Ron Galella//Getty Images

This year's theme was an ode to all things extravagant and those attending didn’t disappoint.

Lady Gaga stunned us all in four custom Brandon Maxwell looks which she showcased via an elaborate which an elaborate strip tease on the red carpet, Zendaya had a real-life Disney princess moment and Katy Perry even dressed as a human burger.
