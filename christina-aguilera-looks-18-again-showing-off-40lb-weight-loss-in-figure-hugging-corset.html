Christina Aguilera'looks 18 again' showing off '40lb weight loss' in figure-hugging corset

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Christina Aguilera may have just won Valentine’s as she shows off her new slender body in a pink corset worn during the Super Bowl parties, just before festivities began. Afterward, she took to social media to celebrate ’14 Valentines’ with her partner, Matthew Rutler.

The music star has been open about her weight loss and has dropped quite a bit of weight over the last few months, recently revealing she’d lost 40lbs over the years. As she embarks on her Las Vegas residency, the 43-year-old is looking better than ever.

Christina Aguilera stuns fans at Super Bowl party

Taking to Instagram to let fans in on her Vegas antics, including the Super Bowl party, the Genie in a Bottle singer left little to the imagination in a tiny pink leotard.

To compliment the look, the star looked stylish in a pair of white knee-high boots as she stuck her tongue out and threw up a ‘reverse peace sign’ to the camera.

In part of the ‘photo dump,’ the singer showcased her friendship with celebs including Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox as they celebrated as the Kansas City Chiefs took the all-important win this weekend.

Of course, fans took to the comments to ‘hype’ the star up, as one penned: “The level of beauty is beyond this world.”

“If somebody told me you were 20 I’d completely believe them,” said another.

A third exclaimed: “You’re the best! You look like you’re 18 again!!”

The star celebrates ’14 Valentines’ with her partner

In a more wholesome post, Xtina celebrated 14 years with her partner, Matt Rutler. In a carousel of photos, the couple kissed and cuddled, in what looked like was backstage at her Vegas residency.

The pair actually got engaged on Valentine’s in 2014 and welcomed their daughter Summer that same year. However, they’re still in no rush to tie the knot.

In 2022, a source told US Weekly they were in “no rush” to get married.  “[Christina] and Matt are happy the way they are. They’re one of those couples who don’t need a document to prove their love for each other,” the insider claimed.
