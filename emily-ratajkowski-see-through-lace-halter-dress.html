See Emily Ratajkowski Brave the Heat in a See-Through Lace Halter Dress

August 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Emily Ratajkowski's bright and sultry street-style look is a perfect way to beat NYC's late-summer heat.

The model and High Low host was seen out and about yesterday afternoon, wearing a pastel-green dress made of completely see-through lace. The midi-length piece featured a deep halter neckline and a flowing skirt, and her beige bra and underwear perfectly matched her skin tone. She added a pair of strappy black heeled sandals and accessorized minimally, wearing a dainty jeweled necklace and carrying a small black bag along with a white dust bag.

As for her glam, the star wore her red summer hair loose and straight, paired with a natural makeup look and a nude lip.

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

While the model's summer style has included plenty of casual, ab-baring separates paired with cool sneakers, Ratajkowski has also stepped out in a number of stylish and breezy dresses. In early August, she wore a business-casual look comprised of a frilly midi-dress by Reformation and zip-up black leather over-the-knee heeled boots. Her accessories included a small brown leather shoulder bag, black oval sunglasses, and large gold hoop earrings.

Days later, the star brought back several '00s accessory trends while dressed a simple black tank minidress by Aexae. She added a low-slung belt with a large gold buckle, and slouchy knee-high leather boots with a small heel. She completed the all-black look with small oval sunglasses, gold hoops, and a quilted leather shoulder bag with gold hardware.
