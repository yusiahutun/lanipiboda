Wiki/Biography

Nora Arnezeder was born on Monday, 8th May 1989, in Paris, France (age 32; as of 2021). She was to an Austrian father and an Egyptian mother. She has one sibling, Lea Arnezeder, who is herself an actor and director.

Later, Nora spent her early childhood with her family at Aix-en-Provence city. At the age of fourteen, she went to Bali, Indonesia, for a year.

When she was sixteen, she came back to Paris to study singing and acting at local school, Académie Internationale De La Danse Et De Théâtre, Paris. She later attended Cours Florent, Paris, to study acting, dancing, and singing.

To pursue her career in acting, she dropped out of high school. At seventeen years, she made her debut in the French TV series ‘Commissaire Valence’.

Family Background

Her Father’s name is Wolfgang Arnezeder and, he is an Austrian Catholic. While her mother is Piera Schinasi, she is an Egyptian Jew. From her mother’s side, Nora is a descendant of the Sephardic Italian family that migrated to Alexandria before world war. Nora’s sister, Lea Arnezeder, is also an actress recognised for This New Generation (2015), Lilith’s Nights and The Station (2017).

Career

Before stepping into the film industry, Nora performed various stage shows and events. Keeping up with her acting skills, she made her first film debut in the French film ‘Leas Deux Mondes’ as ‘Lyri’ in 2007.

Nora from Les Deux Mondes

Paris 36 (also, Fauborg 36) was the first French film starring Nora Arnezedar as a lead role. She played ‘Douce’, an aspiring singer who auditions to perform at the Chansonia. By delivering exceptional performance, Nora earned the Etoiles d’Or Award for the Best Newcomer. In this film, she performed in the song ‘ Loin de Paname’. This song received a nomination for the best original song at the 82nd Academy Awards.

She made her first Hollywood debut with the film ‘The Words’ in 2012 as Celia. She further appeared in American Safe House (2012), In the clouds (2018), Berserk (2019), Faraway eyes (2020), and Army Of The Dead (2021).

Other Achievements

Apart from acting, she has also succeeded in the job of director. She directed the short film. ‘The Interesting Life Of Paul Chanoui’. She became the brand ambassador of Guerlain’s fragrance ‘Idylle’ (in 2009).

She also sings with a band in Louisiana. In 2010, she sang a re-arrangement cover of the song ‘Singin ‘in the Rain’ as a duet with Féloche.

With acting in films and television, she also did theatre. She performed in plays like Après Tout, si ça Marche… (2012) at Théâtre National de Nice and You Will Remember Me (2018).

Presently she is dating an English actor Guy Burnet. They have both been in a relationship since 2015. They publicly confirmed their relationship at Amazon’s Golden Globe Awards Celebration in 2016.
