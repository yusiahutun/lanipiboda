From toting around wine glasses to sipping from bedazzled flasks at the Grammys, Rihanna has been our bad gal icon for years now.

Fans caught a glimpse of the subtle accessory on Instagram, posting a close-up of the flask bracelet. Bad Gal Riri, of course, liked the post.
