Pop star who posed in suicide vest quits showbiz after 'revenge porn' leaked

January 2024 · 2 minute read

A Pakistani female pop star who shocked fans by posing in a suicide vest has announced she is quitting her singing career after nude photographs and videos of her were leaked onto the internet.

Rabi Pirzada, 30, a Pakistani singer who went viral for the stunt, announced in a tweet on Monday that she was "quitting showbiz".

She added: "May Allah forgive my sins. And soften the hearts of people in my favour."

Her decision to leave entertainment comes days after her nudes went viral.

It isn't clear how the videos and photos were made public, but one theory is it was leaked by a jealous ex-boyfriend as an act of "revenge porn".
