I'm A Celeb former Page 3 girl Jessica-Jane Stafford talks 'dirty bits' in shower scenes

January 2024 · 3 minute read

I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! bombshell Jessica-Jane Stafford went down in show history when she starred in one of the show's most memorable shower scenes.

Appearing on the 2011 edition of the hit jungle series, the 34-year-old beauty battled against the likes of Dougie Poynter and TOWIE 's Mark Wright.

Speaking exclusively to Daily Star Online, the former Page 3 model opened up about her time on the show – and her bikini reveals in the jungle plunge pools.

Jessica-Jane admitted that the nation watching from home as you strip to a bikini and have a scrub is bizarre.

She said: "All you want to do is give your bits a really good wash but you don't want that to be seen otherwise it looks like you had dirty bits in the first place!"

She explained: "I quickly fell victim of the 'She's had too much work done' police. One of my photos was analysed by a surgeon saying what I'd had done and it was all wrong, it was totally made up!"

The model and actress, who rose to fame on the hit BBC trickster show The Real Hustle in 2006, said the hardest part was making friends with the fellow celebs.

Reflecting on their reunion, she said: "It was surreal seeing him and hearing about everything he'd seen on telly, you forget that everyone was watching!! He also took me shopping and treated me to loads of bits and it was great to have a kiss and a cuddle that night!"
