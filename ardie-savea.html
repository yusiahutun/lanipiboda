Ardie Savea is a New Zealand personality and a rugby union player. He plays as a flanker and wears the number 8. Ardie played for Wellington from 2012 to 2023 and the Hurricanes from 2013 to 2023. He will start playing for Kobelco Kobe Steelers from the beginning of the new season – 2024.

Ardie also plays international Rugby games for the New Zealand All Blacks. He was a member of the 2019 Rugby World Cup squad and the nominee for the player of the year 2019. Previously, he was the captain of Wellington and played for the Barbarian F.C., for the New Zealand U20’s team.
