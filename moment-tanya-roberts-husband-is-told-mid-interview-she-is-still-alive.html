Tanya Roberts Dies the Day After Her Death is Widely Reported

News of her death the first time proved inaccurate.

update 1/5/2021 6:55 AM

Tanya Roberts was pronounced dead on Monday night.

Her partner confirmed to TMZ that doctors called him just after 9 PM to inform him she had passed away.

A bizarre turn of events saw news of the "That 70s Show" star's death spread around the world early Monday, even though she was still alive at the time.

original story 1/4/2021 3:27 PM

Tanya Roberts' grieving partner found out his wife was still alive in the middle of an interview about her death.

Lance O'Brien was speaking to Inside Edition on camera when he received a phone call from the hospital, informing him the actress was, in fact, not dead.

The sad news that the Bond girl and Charlie's Angel had passed away at the age of just 65 made headlines across the globe on Monday after her publicist confirmed it.
