She may be 42-years-old, but Jennifer Aniston is still proving she can cut it alongside women half her age.

The former Friends actress looks stunning as she poses topless for a new advertisement for Smart Water.

With a hand covering her modesty and her hair loose down her back, the star looks sultry as she stares directly into the camera's lens.

Classy: A stunning Jennifer Aniston poses topless for a new advert for Smartwater

The new image is the latest in a series of recent promotional shots taken of the actress for the designer water, which is enhanced with electrolytes. 

One photograph, released last month, shows the star reclining on a lounger with sun-kissed hair and skin.

Another shows Aniston on a glamorous night out wearing a stunning little black dress while stepping out of a car.

Earlier this month, she took part in a viral video to promote the water, that was jokingly marketed as 'Jen Aniston's Sex Tape'.

Girl next door: Aniston flaunts sun-kissed hair and skin as she poses for a shot released last month for the new Smartwater ad campaignion

Refreshing night on the town: Jennifer gets her gladrags on as she poses for another picture in a sexy little black dress

Best of friends: Aniston, pictured with Cox last June, has denied a rift with her former Friends co-star

In it she plays with a room full off Labrador puppies, and she is told by a grinning team of internet geeks how she could increase the appeal of the video.

Aniston has been a spokesman for the brand since 2007.

Meanwhile, as the new sexy image was released, the actress was forced to deny rumours she has fallen out with best friend Courteney Cox.

According to a report in this week's in Us Weekly magazine, the pair have been experiencing 'tension' since Cox's separation from David Arquette in September last year.

The Us report quoted an insider as saying 'Jen thinks Courteney is being callous' by spending time with Cougartown co-stars Brian Van Holt and Josh Hopkins, without formally filing for divorce from Arquette.

But there are no truth to the claims, according to a spokesman for Aniston.

'It's completely false - there was no falling out,' a representative for the actress told the Los Angeles Times via email.

'Jennifer has always and continues to consider Courteney a dear and close friend.'

Aniston will next appear as a sex-starved dentist in the black comedy movie Horrible Bosses alongside Kevin Spacey, Jason Bateman, Colin Farrell and Jamie Foxx.

The movie's premise follows a trio of long-suffering friends who team up to murder their respective overbearing bosses with disastrous results.
