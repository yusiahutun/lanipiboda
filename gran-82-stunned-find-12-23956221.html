Gran, 82, stunned to find 12-inch Rambo machete in sofa she bought on Facebook

July 2024 · 3 minute read

A grandmother was horrified after finding a 12-inch Rambo machete in a sofa she bought on Facebook.

Shirley McNally, 82, said the fearsome blade weighed a tonne and could “chop somebody's legs right off”.

And she didn’t even make the discovery until a year after the purchase.

She bought the furniture for £180 after seeing it advertised on the social media site in March last year.

The grandmother-of-two decided to sell it earlier this month, so gave it a quick hoover and spray with Febreze before it was collected.

As she cleaned she felt something hard - and found the vicious knife stashed under one of the cushions.
