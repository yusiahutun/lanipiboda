Deestroying is a Costa Rican-American YouTuber who has a net worth of $5.59 million. 

He is known for videos demonstrating his kicking abilities and sports-related skits and vlogs discussing his life.

The YouTuber first gained significant attention as the University of Central Florida placekicker for the National Collegiate Athletic Association. In 2017, the NCAA  told him to delete his YouTube channel if he wanted to stay on the UCF football team, despite his University being okay with it.

The NCAA asked him to delete his channel because it forbids its athletes from profiting off their athletic abilities aside from their scholarships. 

Faced with this dilemma, Deestroying ultimately chose to continue his channel instead of playing for the University of Central Florida. Since quitting the team, the YouTuber has amassed 4.74 million subscribers.

What Is Deestroying's Real Name?

The YouTuber Deestroying's real name is Donald De La Haye Jr.

His family immigrated from Costa Rica when the young boy was only seven years old. He spent his youth in Port St. Lucie, Florida. 

He was into football from a young age, and this interest led to him joining the University of Central Florida's football team as their kickoff specialist. He stayed in the position from 2015 to 2016 until his forced removal.

The YouTuber also played as a wide receiver. While at the University of Central Florida, De La Haye Jr studied marketing and was a marketing major. He is currently a member of the e-sports and entertainment team, FaZe Clan.
