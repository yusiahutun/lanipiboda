1. He memorizes your schedules and routines

Taurus men are known to memorize the daily schedules and routines of ladies. This kind of man is not the best when it comes to displaying bold actions in love which is why they prefer to use a more subtle approach. He will seek your attention by showing up unexpectedly in your life.

It may be at the bus stop in the morning, during a lunch break or even in yoga. A Taurus man is shy and will take time to admire you from far before making his move. Take his admiration of you as a compliment as this is one of the obvious signs that a Taurus man likes you.

He will show you that you mean the world to him by opening up when he is around you because this is his only chance to get to know you better.

Such a man will talk louder when around his close friends, but it will take some courage for him to make himself known in your presence. Men of this zodiac sign will do everything they can to find a topic of interest to get your attention. He will put effort to know more about you and will give you plenty of compliments regarding your looks as well.
