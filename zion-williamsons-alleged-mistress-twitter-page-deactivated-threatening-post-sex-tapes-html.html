Moriah Mills has Twitter suspended after Zion Williamson sex tape threats

August 2024 · 6 minute read

Moriah Mills, the porn star accusing New Orleans Pelicans star Zion Williamson of an affair, had her Twitter account suspended on Tuesday following her threats to release sex tapes of the pair. 

A matter of hours after her Twitter disappeared, the Instagram account attributed to Mills also appeared to be taken down. 

'NBA I have sex tapes of me and Zion Williamson and he also has them on his trap phone @NBA @PelicansNBA,' Mills wrote on Twitter before her account was suspended. 'Trade him now he doesn’t deserve to be in New Orleans !!! Sex tapes dropping soon.' 


It has been two long weeks for the New Orleans Pelicans star Williamson since Mills, a 32-year-old porn star and Only Fans model, started calling him out on Twitter.

Williamson's issues began on June 6, when he and his girlfriend announced they were having a baby, prompting Mills to respond on social media.

'Better pray I'm not pregnant too because I'm definitely late @Zionwilliamson,' Mills tweeted, sparking the ongoing scandal.

She claimed she has been involved with Williamson since 2021 and referred to his girlfriend Ahkeema as 'a low budget porn star.'

The spat took a sinister twist the next day when Mills said culpability lies with Williamson if she is endangered after alleging she has been threatened by a relative of his girlfriend, Ahkeema.

'I'm being harassed now @Zionwilliamson,' she said. 'I don't like didn't threaten anyone if anything happens to me in Atlanta before December. This is getting out of hand. You need to call your people's (sic) Zion thanks I'm done.' 

The early stages of the row caused her name to gain some traction, though. Searches of 'Moriah Mills' on Pornhub rose by 2,293 percent in the wake of the scandal.

Williamson, 22, has not responded directly to Mills but did appear to be in damage control mode in the days after Mills made her claims.

The Pelicans star donated $250,000 to a public school system in Louisiana. The Zion Williamson Foundation donated the huge sum to students enrolled at Jefferson Parish Schools in the outskirts of New Orleans, Louisiana, as part of their Summer Bridge program, according to ESPN.

During the charitable event, however, Williamson's stepfather, Anderson Lee, gave an unexpected speech, indirectly addressing rumors involving Mills.

'I wanna say this,' Lee said in front of a crowd at Jefferson Parish. 'And I wanna be politically correct when I say it. That's my son and I love him.

'Believe half of what you see and nothing you hear. That's all I want to say,' he added. 'I stand by my son one hundred percent. I wasn't always where I am today.'

But it wasn't enough for Mills to stay quiet. Instead, she claimed Williamson got his girlfriend pregnant while she was recovering from failed 'butt-reduction surgery'.

She said: 'I spoke with him and he wants me to delete all the tweets but you had a baby on me @Zionwilliamson you need to man up and fixed this situation get a dna test so we can all move forward with are lives.

'I wish I woulda came and seen you on Valentine's Day as you wanted me to but I was still sick from my failed butt reduction surgery in December so I was on bed rest until March as requested from my doctor. Now look a due date in November.'

In a series of tweets on June 11, Mills posted screen shots of alleged text message conversations she had with Williamson - claiming Zion 'ruined' her life and that she 'genuinely loved' him.

Some of these messages - allegedly from Zion - ask Mills to 'delete everything' the adult entertainer has posted about him. 

She also claimed her fury comes from being lied to by Williamson and not because he is with somebody else.

Mills posted multiple screen shots of conversations over text message - allegedly with Zion

At other times, Mills has appeared tired of the attention she has received, writing once on Twitter: Use (sic) to watch basketball wives on tv when I was younger now the drama has become my life not what I signed up for'.

But Mills has been tweeting relentlessly since, claiming and perhaps her most remarkable claims came in a 20-tweet storm this week.

She threatened to leak sex tapes with him, claimed he has erectile dysfunction and repeatedly called for him to send her money.

'NBA I have sex tapes of me and Zion Williamson and he also has them on his trap phone @NBA @PelicansNBA. Trade him now he doesn’t deserve to be in New Orleans !!! Sex tapes dropping soon,' she wrote online to her 1.1million followers.

She then followed that tweet up with: 'Zion can’t win a case against me I have physical proof that he sent people to threaten me and he know this as well.

'@Zionwilliamson I’m taking you to court for all the damages you have caused my life.'

Mills also posted various screenshots of alleged conversations between the pair in which they discussed him sending her money and offered to book her a flight.

The adult film star continued her online tirade at the NBA star on Twitter Friday 

Mills' latest Twitter rampage included 20 messages in the space of a couple of hours

In the caption accompanying the screenshots, Mills wrote: 'Where is my wire transfer Zion Williamson, you a liar and a cheat where the f*** is my money,' before including the Pelicans and urging them to 'trade this fraud'. 

In multiple other tweets, she claimed 'it's over for you' and insisted his 'NBA career light will be dimmed once the sex tapes release.' 

Williamson has still yet to make comment of any kind on the social media hysteria. Last week, another woman named Yamille Taylor blasted the 22-year-old, calling him a 'sex addict.' 

Williamson has not responded directly to any of this and nor have the Pelicans.
