Who is pastor Ezekiel? Background story, wife, church, and family

Pastor Ezekiel Odero is a famous and influential Kenyan preacher. He heads the New Life Church, based in Mombasa, and has been trending on various social media platforms due to the huge crowds that usually attend his church service.

Pastor Ezekiel pulled a huge crowd (rumoured to be over 50,000) at the Kasarani Stadium for a crusade in November 2022. Amongst those in attendance was Pastor Dorcas Rigathi, the wife of the Kenyan Deputy President, which further bolstered his reputation. So, what is Ezekiel's background? What's his story, and how did he become a famous preacher?

Early life

Pastor Ezekiel Odero was born around the 1970s - he is likely in his 40s or 50s as of 2023. He grew up in Rusinga Island, Homabay County, in Kenya. He came from a poor background and was raised by his mother and grandfather.

Ezekiel Odero started his education at the Ngodhe Island Primary School in Rusinga. However, due to his family's poor status, he usually went fishing at night – at his mother's urging – to supplement his family's income. The overnight activity significantly affected his studies, leading to poor performance at school. When he sat for his KCPE exams, he only managed 210 marks out of a possible 700.

Ezekiel's grandfather, Daniel Odero, a former Kenya Wildlife Service employee, moved with him to Seme in Kisumu, hoping to change his life. He enrolled him at Yeye Primary School, where he would focus on his studies. As a result, he passed when he re-sat his KCPE exams, which earned him admission to Miwani High School in Chemelil, Kisumu.

After four years at Miwani High School, Ezekiel sat for his KCSE examinations, scoring a mean grade of C. He then travelled to Mombasa, where he joined the Mombasa Polytechnic (now the Technical University of Mombasa), pursuing a course in Chemical Processing Technology. Unfortunately, he dropped out of the university in his second year due to financial problems.

Ezekiel Odero was raised in a strict Christian family. He attended the Seventh-Day Adventist Church, where he was in the choir. He also learned to play the keyboard at the church. His Christian background helped set him towards a career in the church ministry years later.

Career

Pastor Ezekiel is rumoured to have worked in construction after moving to Mombasa following his high school studies. While at Mombasa, he also got involved with the Maximum Miracle Church, headed by Bishop Pius Muiru.

Ezekiel Odero started as a choir teacher and a keyboard player before being promoted to praise and worship teacher. During his tenure at the church, he became one of Bishop Muiru's spiritual children, and the preacher (Ezekiel) has revealed that he often turns to him when faced with a problem.

While in Mombasa, Ezekiel Odero met Pastor Sarah, his future wife, who was looking for people to join her in opening a church. Seeing that they shared a similar vision, the two joined hands and opened their church, the New Life Church, in Shanzu.

Over time, Pastor Ezekiel's church grew, becoming a huge attraction to Christian worshippers in Mombasa. However, after 15 years, the preacher moved his church from Shanzu to Mavueni, Kilifi county, his wife's village. He purchased a 65-acre parcel of land, where he built a mega-church on 6.5 acres piece of the land, which is rumoured to have a capacity of 45,000 people.

Besides the church, Pastor Ezekiel has been constructing several other facilities on his purchased land. He is building a four-storey flat to accommodate church employees and their family members. He is also constructing a school and a university, cottages for church guests, a supermarket, restaurants, a hospital, and a rehab centre.

Wife and children

Pastor Ezekiel Odero is married to Pastor Sarah Odero, his wife and partner in his church ministry. The two met at Mombasa when Ezekiel was working as a mason. Their relationship blossomed into romance after spending time together, leading Ezekiel Odero to propose marriage.

The start of their relationship was, however, anything but easy. Ezekiel Odero revealed that his family opposed the marriage and argued that Pastor Sarah – who dropped out of primary school – was uneducated. However, he pressed on, insisting that he only saw her beauty and the love he had for her.

On top of his family's opposition, Ezekiel and Sarah also faced another problem – financial difficulty. At the time, the young pastor was still struggling, and he revealed how he could not afford much when he went to ask for his future wife's hand in marriage. Instead, he only took with him two goats and a Ksh 15 ring. At first, Sarah's family were reluctant but later agreed to the marriage.

Ezekiel and Sarah Odero have been married for over ten years and have three children. Two of the children are their biological kids, while one son is adopted – after his brother's wife died giving birth. The couple and their children live in Mavueni, in the same compound where the pastor's church is.
