Super Bowl: Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce serenade each other at the after party

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Okay, Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce can’t get any cuter, it’s official, especially after seeing the duo at the Super Bowl after party. The pair were seen serenading each other as they danced the night away after the Chiefs’ major win.

If you told Swifties this time last year they wouldn’t want to miss the Super Bowl for the world, they’d laugh at you – but that’s the Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce effect, especially if we take the after party as a case study. The celebrity couple is the most talked about relationship on the planet. Whether it’s body language experts decoding their PDA-filled celebrations, to Taylor’s major signs the relationship is getting ‘serious’, we can’t get enough. Even though the Kansas City Chiefs managed to pull through in the final moments of the game to take home the trophy, we’re still obsessed with the singer and her new beau. New videos show the couple celebrating the team receiving another set of Super Bowl rings, and it’s everything we could have asked for.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce serenade each other at Super Bowl after party

There are always haters, lest not forget trolls who claimed the celebrity was ruining the game, when in reality Taylor is the ‘best thing to happen to the NFL’.

One way to Shake It Off is to just keep on living your best life, and that’s what they’re doing.

Clips of Travis Kelce and Taylor serenading each other across the room is exactly what we expect of a genuine connection. Some cruel rumors from earlier this year claimed it was all a publicity stunt, but they’re proving them wrong every cute photo and video that comes our way.

Even football fans chimed in this weekend, with Chiefs Kingdom fans claiming Taylor was ‘bad luck’ for the team. Well, that’s the end of that debate finally.

In the first video, we see Taylor and Travis dancing and singing to her own single, Love Story, in a club. Then she wraps her arms around him and they kiss. She may be tired of hearing her own songs as her Eras Tour continues, but she probably didn’t mind this time.

Then another of her tracks comes on, You Belong With Me, and the pair are pointing to each other across the club. The tight end is behind the decks with the DJ, loving the music – he is a true fan, of course.

Taylor lifts her drink in a toast to the 34-year-old as they gush over each other despite being so far apart.

Love is in the air

Fans adored seeing the moment between them, with one Twitter user pointing out: “Taylor Swift is the reason 50% of people watched the Super Bowl.”

A second simply said: “Well, she’s happy,” and a third chimed in: “It’s the season of love.”

Another added: “Swifties and Chiefs fans unite! Is this the mashup we never knew we needed?”

But one was feeling emotional: “I want what they have,” as they added a crying emoji.
