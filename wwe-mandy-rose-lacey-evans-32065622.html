Former NXT women’s champion, Rose, has made no secret that since leaving her former career behind the money she has made has been “life changing”.

Rose, aka Amanda Saccomanno, was fired from WWE in 2022 after the organisation was allegedly ‘unhappy’ with the content she was sharing on her private platforms.

It was reported that Rose was not even given an opportunity to take down her premium content before her WWE release.

Since her departure Rose has spoken out about her lucrative career change with her most recent partnership with OnlyFans.

Speaking to TMZ, Rose said: “It's been kind of life-changing, I must say ... I'm really blessed, but I will say that I have a lot of financial freedom right now which is really amazing."

Meanwhile Lacey, who now goes by her real name Macey Estrella, has also taken to adult content creation, and recently teased a live show.

With 1.4 million followers on Instagram she has no shortage of viewers either.

The 33-year-old had a stop-start WWE career, with several different gimmicks since her debut in 2016.

She was even involved in a random storyline where she claimed she was pregnant with the baby of 74-year-old Ric Flair, before disappearing off-screen to go and have an actual child in 2021 with her real husband Alfonso Estralla-Kadlec.

In August 2023, she looked to have left the company, having removed WWE from all of her social media and changing her account names to Limitless Macey.

A post she made at the time of her departure saw her sharing a photo of her wearing denim hot pants and a Lacey Evans jacket, with the comment: “When the clock strikes 12, you will address me as Macey Estrella from here on out.
