    • Group: Faction Missions

    Faction: Ryujin Industries

    This mission starts automatically once you've completed the previous Ryujin mission - "A New Narrative". Talk to Imogene and you will find out what task she has for you this time.

    I should speak to Imogene to find out what my next assignment is.

    Prior missions

    To begin Access is Key you must first complete A New Narrative.

    Objectives of Access is Key

    1. (Optional) Wear a Suit or Security Guard Uniform

    I need to obtain a Security Keycard and do my best not to get caught. If I want the Security Chief to speak to me, I should wear a suit or security uniform. I can buy a suit from Aito in the Ryujin Tower lobby.

    Having the right outfit is essential as long as you want to try to convince the Security Chief to give you the security keycard. If you are not dressed appropriately he will not want to talk to you.

    You can buy the outfit from Aito Suzuki who you will meet in the lobby of Ryujin Tower.

    Buy any of the corpo suits, then wear it.

    2. Travel to [Security Chief Location]

    I need to travel to [Security Chief Location] on [Security Chief Location - Planet] in the [Security Chief Location - System] System to obtain a Security Keycard. If I want the Security Chief to speak to me, I should wear a suit or security uniform. I can buy a suit from Aito in the Ryujin Tower lobby.

    Where the Security Chief is located is random so if it is on a planet other than Volii Alpha then you must first go to that planet. In my case I did not have to travel far because he was on Volii Alpha. In my case I did not have to fly anywhere because he was on Volii Alpha.

    3. Retrieve the Security Keycard

    I need to obtain a Security Keycard in [Security Chief Location] and do my best not to get caught. If I want the Security Chief to speak to me, I should wear a suit or security uniform. I can buy a suit from Aito in the Ryujin Tower lobby.

    When you reach the place where the Security Chief is located you will be able to try to get the keycard in one of the following ways:

    1. You can use your pickpocketing skills and steal the keycard.

    2. Talk to him, and this will only be possible if you have the right suit on, and try to persuade him to give you the keycard.

    3. The last option would be to kill him, but this is highly inadvisable as you will have to kill all the guards and a high bounty will be put on your head. Imogene will also mention that this is not what she expected, which means you will receive half the credits.

    4. Return to Imogene at Ryujin Tower

    I successfully obtained the Security Keycard. I need to return to Imogene at Ryujin Tower to hand it over.

    To complete this mission, return to Imogene and hand her the keycard. You will receive 100 XP and credits for doing so. The next mission - "Sowing Discord" - will automatically begin.

    I was able to acquire a Security Keycard for Imogene Salzo.

    After completing Access is Key you will get or will be able to get Sowing Discord.
