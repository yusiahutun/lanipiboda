Some Like It Hot Tops The List As The 2023 Tony Awards Nominations Are Announced

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — On Tuesday, The American Theatre Wing and the Broadway League announced the nominees for the 76th Annual Antoinette Perry “Tony” Awards.

Leading the charge for 2023 is “Some Like It Hot” a musical adaptation of the iconic cinematic comedy that featured Jack Lemmon, Tony Curtis and Marilyn Monroe.

Featuring songs by Scott Wittman and Marc Shaiman, the musical tells the story of two friends who disguise themselves as women to join an all-girl band after witnessing a mob hit. The stage production, which takes on new resonance in the era of challenges to trans rights, received 13 nominations, including Best Direction of a Musical and Best Choreography for director Casey Nicholaw, and Best Musical.

Contenders for Best New Play include Tom Stoppard’s “Leopoldstadt,” an intergenerational story about a Jewish family, and Fat Ham,” James Ijames’ Pulitzer Prize-winning take on Hamlet, which takes place at a Black family’s barbecue in the Southern U.S.

Nominees for 2023 were selected by The nominees were selected by an independent committee of 40 theatre professionals appointed by the Tony Awards Administration Committee with nominees drawn from theatrical productions opening in any of the 41 eligible Broadway theatres during the current season.

The Tony Awards, hosted by Ariana DeBose, will air LIVE on Sunday, June 11, 2023 from the historic United Palace in Washington Heights, in New York City from 8:00-11:00 PM, ET/5:00-8:00 PM, PT on the CBS Television Network, and streaming live and on demand on Paramount+.

For the CBS broadcast, Ricky Kirshner and Glenn Weiss/White Cherry Entertainment are Executive Producers. Weiss also serves as Director.
