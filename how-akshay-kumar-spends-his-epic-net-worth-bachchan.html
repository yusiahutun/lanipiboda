How Akshay Kumar spends his epic net worth: the Bachchan Pandey actor loves pampering wife Twinkle K

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The Mumbai villa

If you follow Akshay’s wife Twinkle Khanna on Instagram, you’ll definitely have caught sight of the couple’s luxurious Arabian Sea-facing villa. According to GQ India, the house has a huge living room area, home theatre, big kitchen and, naturally, a walk-in wardrobe.

BollywoodShaadis reports the house cost around US$10.5 million and is located in the swanky Juhu area, an upmarket neighbourhood of Mumbai.

According to Vogue India, the garden area of the villa is notable because the plot originally belonged to Sumati Morarjee, a close friend of Mahatma Gandhi. There is a raised stone structure in the garden, which is untouched because this is where Gandhi used to meditate during his visits.

The house also reportedly boasts an indoor pond, antique silver candelabras, and an impressive 13-part pendant light installation by Klove Studio.

The luxury cars

According to BollywoodShaadis, Kumar owns a Rolls-Royce Phantom VII, which is one of the most expensive out there, coming in at over US$1 million.

The interior roof of the car reportedly looks like a starry night sky thanks to more than 1,300 starlight headliners and the inner leatherwork is known to be of the best quality. Kumar also owns a Mercedes-Benz V-Class, a Bentley Continental GT and a Harley-Davidson.

Multiple properties

Not content with his seaside villa, the actor also owns four flats on the 21st floor of the elegant Transcon Triumph building in Mumbai, according to GQ India. It’s believed that the flats cost him around almost US$600,000 each. Oh, and he also has a villa in Goa for good measure.

The Ahmedabad Mirror described the villa as Portuguese-style, and it has a private pool. The actor and his family are often spotted in the area on holiday and are rumoured to consider it their second home.

Treats for Twinkle

Kumar is known to drop huge amounts of money on his wife, Twinkle Khanna. The couple got married in 2001 and the actor has never been reluctant to show how much he loves her by buying her whatever her heart desires. Including once surprising Khanna with a Bentley Continental.

In an interview with Koffee with Karan, Khanna said that when the couple first got together, Kumar often used to forget to buy her birthday gifts. He apparently once gave her a paperweight and she joked that when the time was right, he could buy her a diamond the size of the paperweight. Twinkle said she got her wish and is often seen flashing the rock on her finger.

A private jet

Who wants to go to the trouble of hiring a private plane when you have the money to just buy one? Well, that’s what Kumar did at least. BollywoodShaadis reports that the star mostly uses his private jet to get around when doing promos for films.

He has also been known to use it to take his family on holidays – so they can jet off to exotic locations whenever they desire.
