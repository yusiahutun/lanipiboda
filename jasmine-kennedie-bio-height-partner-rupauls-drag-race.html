Jasmine Kennedie On RuPaul’s Drag Race

Does Jasmine Kennedie Have A Partner?

Yes, Jasmine Kennedie had a partner named Michael Burns before she appeared on RuPaul’s Drag Race in 2022. He is an Elmira, New York, native, who worked as an instructor at Chelsea Piers Fitness. Also, the guy previously worked as a lifeguard at YWCA Elmira and the Twin Tiers.

Find him over IG @michaelburns._ and FB @Michael Burns.

The two were together at least since 2017 and celebrated their anniversary on May 19.

Looking back on their journey, Jasmine and Michael were super involved in each other lives. They’ve attended various functions together, voted for Biden together, and even celebrated Jasmine’s sister’s graduation together.

Overall, they were a dream couple of many.

Trivia: Michael celebrates his birthday on August 18, and is of the Leo zodiac.

Jasmine Kennedie Job

Jasmine Kennedie is a drag queen by profession. Pre-pandemic, she worked 4-5 nights a week, and now, though the gigs are less, she was able to “adapt” and still maintain her job as a drag performer.

This self-labeled “backflipping bimbo” from New York City, Jasmine always dreamed of going to Hollywood. So, she learned how to make up from her sister and Youtube videos and started from there. Over time, with more experimentation, she then finally realized her gift. “I feel I paint more on the ‘old way’ of drag: a lot of powder, and seven different colors for contour. Taking off my makeup takes as much effort as getting to the center of a Tootsie Pop,” she said.

Within no time, she got so popular that she even was featured in the Cosmopolitan magazine before she appeared on RuPaul’s Drag Race in 2022.

However, Jasmine wasn’t always into the arts. She was more of an athletic person. She played soccer for eight years, swam and dove for three played lacrosse, and even ran a cross country track.

“But I used to always play in my mom’s makeup and clothes as any drag queen did,” she recalled.

Also, she was often involved with cheerleading at school.

Jasmine Kennedie Family

Jasmine Kennedie comes from a Polish American family of four. She was born to parents, Joy Bell and Rick Koritkowski, and has a sister named Carlee Koritkowski.

Her father, Rick is a Connecticut native, who loved playing basketball and dancing in a Polish dance group. However, after high school, he went on to begin his career in bartending and worked there almost all his life.

As for her mother, Jasmine shared that Joy was the “best mom anyone could ask for.” Also, recalling the time her mother found out that she did drag, Jasmine shared, “She said ‘if you’re going to be a drag queen. you’re gonna be a classy one.’”

Great job, Mom!

Also, Joy Bell celebrates her birthday on March 20.

Talking about her sister, Carlee is a nature enthusiast who studied Ecology at SUNY Plattsburgh. As of 2022, she lived in  Salt Lake City, Utah working at the Student Conservation Association.

Did you know: Jasmine’s drag mother is Mancie Mandell.
