Best WWE divas: 10 female wrestlers you should watch in 2022

March 2024 · 7 minute read

When the WWE introduced the term diva to refer to female performers in the 1990s, it created a division that was about to entertain fans for many years. But even before the 1990s, women in WWE were already trending and making headlines in the mainstream media. From the Fabulous Moolah to Velvet McIntyre, and Princess Victoria, the WWE has seen some of the strongest women wrestlers in history. But who are the best WWE divas to watch out for in 2022?

The WWE has over 100 women wrestlers in its books. But unlike before, when in-ring talent was not a concern for women wrestlers, the current group of divas can showcase their talents and even engage in some feuds to make their storylines more entertaining.

The best WWE divas have transformed the women's division and the entire wrestling fraternity. They have also made it possible for fans to look forward to electrifying main event matches like WrestleMania, WWE SmackDown, and RAW Women's Championships.

Who are the best WWE divas in 2022?

Although the WWE introduced the word diva in the 1990s to refer to its female performers, including wrestlers, managers or valets, backstage interviewers, or ring announcers, it dropped the word in 2016.

The wrestling promotion started referring to women as Superstars. But the word diva has stuck in fans' minds for decades, and they have associated themselves with specific female wrestlers. According to Ranker, here are the best WWE divas in 2022:

1. Alexa Bliss

Alexa Bliss joined WWE's SmackDown roster in 2016. Less than a year after her debut, she became the first Superstar to win SmackDown Women's Championship and RAW Women's Championship.

The two wins made her set the record of the first woman to hold two titles from different divisions twice. Alexa is undoubtedly one of the best Wrestlemania divas to watch in 2022, mainly because of her talents, natural in-ring movements, and fluidity.

2. Asuka

Asuka is famous for marching to the ring in a traditional noh mask and kaleidoscopic battle gear. Many WWE fans love her because she is fearless and uses her techniques to take down her opponents.

While wrestling, her intimidating looks enabled her to become a two-time WWE RAW Women's Champion and one-time WWE SmackDown Women's Champion. She also became the winner of the first-ever Women's Royal Rumble Match in 2018.

3. Becky Lynch

Becky Lynch is an exceptional wrestler and one of the best women in WWE history. She has proven countless times that she is the best in business by winning many titles since making her WWE debut.

Lynch became the inaugural SmackDown Women's Champion at Backlash 2016 and has held the title four times. But her technical ability to finish off her opponents makes her stand out from the rest.

4. Bianca Belair

Bianca Belair is one of the hottest WWE female wrestlers. But apart from her general looks, she has about everything one would look for in a wrestler. She is strong, athletic, agile, and entertaining.

After the WWE drafted her to SmackDown in 2021, Belair used her competitive nature to win a Royal Rumble match and successfully challenged for the SmackDown Women's Championship at WrestleMania 37. Belair is a force to reckon with in the WWE, and she is only getting started.

5. Charlotte Flair

Charlotte Flair is the daughter of Ric Flair, a 16-time World Champion. Since joining the WWE, she has lived up to her father's name. However, Charlotte took a different path from her father's and became one of the best overall female WWE Superstars in history.

She has defeated some of the top names in WWE history, including defeating Nikki Bella to win the WWE divas Championship. Furthermore, she is a six-time WWE RAW and SmackDown Women's Champion.

6. Liv Morgan

Liv Morgan is a name that should be on every list of wrestling fans as a future leader of the WWE's women's division. In 2022, Liv Morgan won the Women's Money in the Bank ladder match and later cashed in to defeat Ronda Rousey, ultimately winning the SmackDown Women's Championship.

Although it was her first-ever title in her career, Morgan has shown on many occasions that she is a competitive wrestler. She combines agility and creativity to win her matches.

7. Naomi

Naomi is a two-time SmackDown Women's Champion after winning the blue belt twice in 2017. When she debuted in 2012, people saw her as a wrestler with extraordinary athleticism.

She then went on a remarkable run as a fan-favourite and corner-cutting villain. Her efforts made her win the WWE Women's Tag Team Championship at WrestleMania 38 alongside another fierce competitor, Sasha Banks.

8. Rhea Ripley

Rhea Ripley won the RAW Women's Championship at WrestleMania 37. But one of the factors that made her a champion is that she is a powerhouse and agile. Ripley is also one of the few divas to watch out for not only in 2022 but in future.

Her young age and ultra-aggressive and terrifyingly strong nature make her a competitive wrestler who can take down any opposition.

9. Ronda Rousey

Ronda Rousey is one of the best WWE diva wrestlers of all time. When she won her first match at WrestleMania 34 in 2018, Rousey participated in 30 wrestling encounters in the WWE and only lost two matches.

She then dominated her opponents and became a one-time WWE SmackDown and RAW Women's Champion. Rousey can get into any situation and come out with a successful outcome. That makes her an outstanding WWE Superstar.

10. Sasha Banks

Sasha Banks is a talented, feisty, and fast wrestler in the WWE. She has made history in the WWE, becoming the first-ever Women's WWE Iron Man, winning Hell in a Cell, Royal Rumble, and Elimination Chamber matches.

Banks has earned the respect of many WWE fans and colleagues because of her confidence and brings legitimacy to the women's division of the WWE.
