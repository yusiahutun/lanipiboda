June 2024 · 2 minute read

They say friendships made inside the Bigg Boss house often do not last. However, a few people in the history of the show proved this wrong. There is one group of friends who have been paly with each other even after the controversial show ended. If you still haven't guessed them, it is none other than Rajiv Adatia, Tejasswi Prakash, and Karan Kundrra.

Whenever this trio meets, there is laughter and fun, and they exude the same energy that they shared inside the Bigg Boss 15 house. The trio recently bonded at an event and reminisced old memories.

Rajiv Adatia, Tejasswi Prakash and Karan Kundrra's reunion

At an event in Bandra, Bigg Boss 15's popular trio Rajiv Adatia, Tejasswi Prakash, and Karan Kundrra reunited after a long time. The trio bonded pretty well inside the Bigg Boss 15 house and has been in touch with each other. Be it birthdays or any other special occasions, they stay connected. At the event, the trio recalled their memories from the reality show as Karan held Tejasswi and kissed her. For the uninitiated, Kundrra displayed his affection for his ladylove in the same way inside the controversial reality show to irk Adatia. 

Rajiv responded to Karan's antics and said, "Bigg Boss mey bhi aise hi karta tha" (He used to do the same in the Bigg Boss house.)
