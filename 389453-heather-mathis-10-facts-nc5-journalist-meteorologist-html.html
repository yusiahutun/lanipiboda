Heather Mathis: 10 facts about the NC5 journalist and meteorologist

July 2024 · 6 minute read

Many people talk about the weather when there is really nothing else to talk about. It is an easy topic to turn to when the conversation seems to meet a dead end. It's different for Heather Mathis. The journalist and meteorologist is passionate about all things weather.

Heather Mathis knew from an early age what she wanted to pursue as a career. She lives to make sure viewers feel prepared and ready for the day through accurate and informative forecasts. With such a clear cut goal, even the sky cannot limit her from achieving her ambitions.

10 quick facts about the NC5 journalist and meteorologist

The beautiful and eloquent Heather Mathis has made a name for herself in the newsroom. She is a forecaster and also a news reporter. Her zeal for what she does has made her stand out from many others. Here are some facts about the weather girl:

1. Heather Mathis was born on August 15th, 1989

The stunning TV personality celebrated her 31st birthday this year. There is not much public information about her family and siblings, although she makes mention of them on social media. Heather Mathis bio is also a private affair, and there is little or no detail in the limelight. The beautiful weather anchor is relatively short. However, Heather Mathis height is not exactly known.

2. Heather was inspired to pursue Meteorology after a tornado outbreak in her home town

The journalist was raised in Mt Juliet, Tennessee. When she was a young girl, the town was hit by a tornado outbreak in 1998. These events birthed in her a desire to keep her community safe and informed during extreme weather conditions.

This desire set the base for her career mission — to keep viewers informed of accurate and informative weather forecasts to keep them prepared for any conditions that may arise. This passion was developed further through her studies.

3. She holds two Bachelors degrees

The forecaster studied at Mt Juliet High School. Later on, she joined Middle Tennessee State University to study broadcast journalism. While here, she was a weather anchor for the student TV station. She also has a second degree in Atmospheric Sciences and Meteorology from Mississippi State University.

To date, Mathis is still very passionate about giving back to the community and teaching children about weather. She is involved in several school programs. Every once in a while, Heather Mathis weather forecasts feature children so that they can learn.

4. Her first job was at WBBJ-TV in Jackson, Tennessee

Heather's career in journalism was birthed in WBBJ-TV in Jackson, Tennessee. Between September 2012-September 2014, she was a full-time reporter and forecaster. From September 2013-September 2014, she also worked as an anchor.

Afterwards, she moved to WAAY-TV in Huntsville, Alabama where she held the positions of meteorologist and reporter between September 2014 and August 2017. The weather girl also served her last 8 months as an executive producer, from January to August 2017.

5. Heather Mathis husband is called Ryan

The journalist is married to the love of her life, Ryan. In September this year, the couple celebrated their 5th wedding anniversary. The two lovebirds like to spend time together and also travel a lot. They currently reside in Nashville, Tennessee.

6. Heather Mathis Instagram account has more than 10,000 followers

The bubbly TV anchor has an exciting Instagram page where you can find her as Weather Heather. She frequently posts photos of herself at work as well as regular weather updates. She also has several photos taken with children, including her nieces and nephews.
