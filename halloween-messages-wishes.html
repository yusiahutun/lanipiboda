65 Halloween Messages and Wishes for 2023 Cards, Texts and More

You've already figured out costumes for yourself, your hubby, your kids and even your pets, whipped up a batch of terror-ific treats and are about to relax with an on-theme cocktail in front of a scary movie. You've totally nailed the season of the witch, except, well, for one thing: you haven't sent out any Halloween messages yet. October 31 may not be the biggest greeting card holiday of the year, but that doesn't mean fiends and fam won't love getting one with a personal line or two from you. Or, you might want to blast out a text, or tag 'em all in a social media post to spread some All Hallows' Eve fear—er, cheer.

No matter which way you want to reach out to your boo crew, you'll find just the right Halloween message to use below. We have short and sweet-as-trick-or-treat-candy quips great for dashing off via text, chill-inspiring quotes about the day perfect to include in a card and even super-clever wishes and sayings specifically for your S.O. and the littles in your life. And if you like puns (and come on, who doesn't?), we have included a few that'll leave you howling like a werewolf at the full moon. So read on for the best messages to make their Halloween a total scream.
