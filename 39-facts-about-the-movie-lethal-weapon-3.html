FAQs

1. When was Lethal Weapon 3 released?

Lethal Weapon 3 was released on May 15, 1992.

2. Who directed Lethal Weapon 3?

Lethal Weapon 3 was directed by Richard Donner.

3. How many Lethal Weapon movies are there?

There are a total of four Lethal Weapon movies: Lethal Weapon (1987), Lethal Weapon 2 (1989), Lethal Weapon 3 (1992), and Lethal Weapon 4 (1998).

4. Who were the main cast members of Lethal Weapon 3?

The main cast members of Lethal Weapon 3 were Mel Gibson as Martin Riggs, Danny Glover as Roger Murtaugh, and Joe Pesci as Leo Getz.

5. What is the plot of Lethal Weapon 3?

Lethal Weapon 3 follows detectives Martin Riggs and Roger Murtaugh as they investigate a former cop turned weapons dealer who is selling illegal armor-piercing bullets.

6. Did Lethal Weapon 3 receive positive reviews?

Lethal Weapon 3 received mixed to positive reviews from critics. While some praised its action sequences and humor, others felt it lacked the depth of the previous films in the franchise.

7. Are there any memorable quotes from Lethal Weapon 3?

One of the most memorable quotes from Lethal Weapon 3 is Martin Riggs’ line, “I’m too old for this shit,” which became a catchphrase for the character throughout the series.

8. Did Lethal Weapon 3 perform well at the box office?

Yes, Lethal Weapon 3 was a commercial success, grossing over $320 million worldwide against a budget of $35 million.

9. Is there any connection between Lethal Weapon 3 and the TV series adaptation?

Yes, the Lethal Weapon TV series, which aired from 2016 to 2019, was loosely based on the characters and events of the original Lethal Weapon film series.

10. Where can I watch Lethal Weapon 3?

Lethal Weapon 3 is available for streaming on various platforms, including Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and Hulu.
