The Hudson siblings are bringing Sibling Revelry back!

    Kate Hudson and her brother Oliver Hudson have a tight relationship, and they're bringing the greatest showcase of their good-natured ribbing back to the airwaves.

    The siblings took to the Instagram page of their podcast "Sibling Revelry" to announce that the newest season of the show would be out sooner than you'd think!

    On National Siblings Day this Monday, they posted for the first time in nearly a year, writing: "It's National siblings day! AND we'll be back later this week with a brand new season of #SiblingRevelry!!!"

    Their fans were ecstatic about the announcement with one saying: "THIS MADE MY [expletive] YEAR!! I thought I was going to be 95 with 5 grandkids by the time it came back. (I'm now 24)"

    Another wrote: "I'm so excited you'll be back! You guys should do an episode with your other brother too. Can't wait to listen again," and a third added: "Oh thank goodness. I thought this podcast was no more."

    The photographs they posted alongside the news featured a blast from the past, pairing recent shots with childhood throwbacks, including an adorable snap of them from their dinner table as kids looking like a pair of twins.

    "Sibling Revelry" returns April 12

    Goldie Hawn shares Kate, 43, and Oliver, 46, with ex-husband Bill Hudson, who she was married to from 1976 to 1980, before meeting and striking up a relationship with Kurt Russell in 1983. They also have four half-siblings; three from Bill's other relationships plus 36-year-old Wyatt from Goldie and Kurt.

    "Sibling Revelry" will return on April 12 for the start of season three, their first episode in a year, having featured celebrity friends like Matthew McConaughey, Rob Lowe, Jessica Alba, and Nina Dobrev in the past.

    The final episode of the second season featured a plug for Oliver's new podcast with his wife Erinn Hudson titled "Unconsciously Coupled," openly discussing their marriage, life as parents to three kids, and the more hilarious side of being a Hollywood family.

    The official description for "Sibling Revelry" reads: "'Sibling Revelry' explores the sibling bond, family dynamics, the human mind, and so much more. 

    "Kate and Oliver dive deep into the things that interest them and talk to other siblings in a free-formed, wide open, relaxed conversation to not only have some laughs, but to maybe inspire some people along the way with universal tales of what it's like to grow up with brothers and sisters."
