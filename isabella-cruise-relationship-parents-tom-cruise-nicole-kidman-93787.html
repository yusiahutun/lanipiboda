Rumors have swirled for years that Isabella Cruise is estranged from her famous parents, Tom Cruise and Nicole Kidman, but in a recent interview, the 25-year-old confirmed that she has a solid relationship with her both mom and dad today. “Of course we talk, they’re my parents,” she told New Idea magazine, adding that anyone who says otherwise is “full of s–t.” Scroll down to see learn even more about Isabella and her family!

Is Isabella Cruise married?

Yes! Isabella married her husband, Max Parker, in London in September 2015 — but neither Tom, 55, nor Nicole, 50, attended her wedding, further leading to speculation that the young woman is not close with her parents. However, it was later revealed that while Nicole was not invited to her daughter’s nuptials, Tom allegedly paid for the entire event. At the time, several outlets reported that Isabella didn’t want her famous parents at her wedding because she wanted to avoid a media frenzy.

Nicole and Tom with children Isabella and Connor in 1996. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

“[Nicole] is very happy for Bella. She, like all mothers, wants her child to be happy, and it surely seems Bella is very happy,” a source close to the actress said at the time. Tom and Nicole — who were married from 1990 until 2001 — adopted daughter Isabella and son Connor Cruise, 23, shortly after their respective births.

Is Isabella Cruise close with Nicole Kidman today?

After Tom and Nicole’s split, Bella and Conner both went to live with their dad, much to Nicole’s dismay. “They are generous, kind, and hardworking. And these are traits that I love to see in my children. They live with Tom, which was their choice. I’d love them to live with us, but what can you do?” the actress said in a May 2014 interview.

In the years following, Tom welcomed another daughter, Suri Cruise, with his now ex-wife Katie Holmes and Nicole had two girls, Sunday Rose Urban and Faith Margaret Urban, with husband Keith Urban. In a previous interview, Nicole revealed that she often keeps in touch with her older kids via text message.

Life & Style recently revealed that Nicole is now officially back in her daughter’s life thanks to Isabella’s new business venture. When Bella launched her Bella Kidman Cruise clothing line in mid-February, it was the first time she ever publicly used Nicole’s last name, proving the two are now on good terms. “[Nicole and Bella] have put aside any differences they may have had. Nicole is thrilled that Bella has paid tribute to her by naming her clothing line BKC. She’s supportive of Bella’s career move and really excited for her!” a source confessed.

Isabella and Connor in 2013. (Photo Credit: Splash)

What is Isabella Cruise’s net worth?

It’s not totally clear what Isabella’s personal net worth is, but her parents Tom and Nicole are worth a reported $550 million and 130 million, respectively. Despite her parents’ mega wealth, Isabella and her husband — who works as an IT consultant — seemingly live a modest life outside of London. According to The Sun the couple recently moved into a “low-key property” in Croydon, England. Though the “semi-detached” home is thought to be worth around $677,000, Bella and her hubby, Max, reportedly rent a one-bedroom apartment within the house and share the overall property with three other tenants.
