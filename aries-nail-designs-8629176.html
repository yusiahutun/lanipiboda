20 Aries Nail Ideas

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Aries is a fire sign that's known for its fearless yet playful nature. It's associated with bold colors like red, orange, and yellow — so "flames are a great element for Aries nails," says celebrity manicurist Fleury Rose. For a less on-the-nose look, you can take those blazing colors and translate them into different designs. Alternatively, you can lean into statement-making designs in any color palette to speak to the signs' confident nature. Ahead, browse through 20 Aries nail looks to rock this season and beyond.

Flaming Red

Rose created this over-the-top manicure featuring flames, gems, metal appliqués, and more. “I love these flaming-red flames for the ultimate power nails,” says Rose.

Yellow-Orange Aura

This zesty aura nail starts with a rich red-orange on the outside and fades to an electric yellow. It makes a statement without being overly intricate.

Circle Tips

This adventurous and dynamic look perfectly embodies the Aries spirit. It features two-toned circles at the tip of each nail, using bold colors like black, neon green, and coral.

Aura Flames

These pastel aura nails are topped with white flames, creating a look that's both soft and edgy.

Butterflies & Flames

For a striking contrast, Rose toped a shimmery pink polish with neon-green flames and butterflies. “I gave these flames a girly twist by using spring colors, and butterflies,” says Rose.

Bright Swirls

Since Aries are known for their confidence, a bold manicure is right up their alley. This manicure features a funky mix of fuchsia, orange, sky blue, cerulean, maroon, and mustard.

Starburst Skittle

This jelly manicure uses four different colors — yellow, mustard, yellow-orange, and red-orange — to create a juicy skittle look.

Pink & Red Mix ‘n Match

Go for a pink and red look for a bold, romantic look. This mix-and-match manicure features squiggles, dots, cow print, and French tips.

Orange-Red

“I’m a huge fan of a bright orange-red,” says Rose. Atop a clean almond nail, it creates a fiery yet classic look.

Pink Tortoise

Take the tortoise print and spice it up with hot pink accents. This manicure makes use of negative space, mixing tips with clear hearts. To add another layer of interest, the look is finished with a matte top coat.

Happy Tips

With red as the main color associated with Aries, a mani that's made with red and pink is perfect. This one features smiley face tips with the colors alternating from nail to nail.

Strawberries

Aries season coincides with the start of strawberry season, making it the perfect fruit to feature in a springy zodiac-inspired nail look.

Red Chrome

This red chrome nail polish takes an Aries color up a notch with even more pizzaz. The chrome finish reflects light for a dazzling effect.

All Squares

This red-and-orange manicure leans into the square shape of the nails with lots of squares and rectangles. However, the thumb nail has an abstract design, leaning into Aries' playful side.

Coral Florals

For an orange look that's a bit more subdued, opt for a coral hue. The addition of white flowers softens the look even more.

Cranberry

This deep cranberry red has strong Aries energy, effusing bravery and passion.

Spicy Mix

Lean into Aries' bold and fearless side with this fun mix-and-match mani that features a spicy pepper alongside playful hearts and flowers.

Cherries

For a red look that's more sweet than fiery, opt for a design with cherries. The dainty size of the design introduces another layer of softness to the look.

Pink Chrome

Pink chrome is a super fun and adventurous choice, making it perfect for an Aries. It reflects shades of blue and orange to add a ton of interest to the look.
