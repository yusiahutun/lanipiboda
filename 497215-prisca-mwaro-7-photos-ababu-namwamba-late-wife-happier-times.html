Sports Cabinet secretary Ababu Namwamba's ex-wife Prisca Mwaro has passed away at her Nairobi home.

The two had a picturesque relationship before they started having issues in 2018. TUKO.co.ke compiled photos of the duo during happy times.

1. Prisca Mwaro and Namwamba's three kids

Prisca and Namwamba shared three kids, two boys and a girl. Their daughter is the eldest, and the doting mother celebrated her in 2021.

The girl won a school election in Class Six, and a proud Prisca shared the news on social media.

2. Prisca and Nwamba’s humble beginnings

The two former lovebirds started dating when they were still pretty young.

A throwback photo shared by Prisca in 2015 showed them smiling at the camera as Namwamba held her from the back.

From their dressing, the image was from the early 2000s.

3. Prisca and Namwamba on magazine cover

The CS and his ex-wife were once considered one of Kenya’s most lovely couples.

In 2016, they were featured on the cover of Drum Magazine.

In the cover image, the two looked happier than ever before.

4. Prisca and Namwamba at his beach hotel

Namwamba owns the Che’s Bay Resort on the shores of Lake Victoria.

He holds an annual Isambo Festival, and Prisca supports him.

An old photo showed them cheering while watching performances at the festival.
