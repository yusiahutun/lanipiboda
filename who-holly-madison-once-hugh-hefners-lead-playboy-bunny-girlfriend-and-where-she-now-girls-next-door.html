So, who is Holly Madison exactly?

Holly Madison was Hugh Hefner’s girlfriend

Holly Madison dated Playboy magazine publisher Hugh Hefner between 2001 and 2008 whilst living at his Playboy Mansion in LA, according to E! News, and was considered his “lead” or “No 1 girlfriend”. She was reportedly scouted to be a bunny after doing a stint at Hooters restaurant.

She got into reality TV

After leaving the mansion, Madison got into the reality television game. According to her website, she spent five seasons starring on the E! hit television show, The Girls Next Door, and then enjoyed two seasons of her own spin-off series, Holly’s World.

In 2022, Madison dropped a bombshell in the Secrets of Playboy documentary, describing her intimate nights with Hefner as “very mechanical and robotic”, and saying: “I think I definitely thought I was in love with Hef but it was very Stockholm syndrome”.

She’s published more than one memoir

The former Playboy bunny has three books to her name. These include Down The Rabbit Hole, which debuted at No 1 on The New York Times Bestseller list (2015), The Showgirl Next Door: Holly Madison’s Las Vegas (2011), and The Vegas Diaries: Romance, Rolling the Dice, and the Road to Reinvention (2016).

What happened to Holly Madison and Hugh Hefner?

Per E! News, Madison called off the relationship in 2008, years before Hefner’s death in 2017. “I tricked myself into thinking I had feelings for Hef,” she wrote in Down the Rabbit Hole. “He had this gentlemanly veneer, he was intelligent, and I loved watching old movies with him. I focused on the positive things, not the negative.”

As reported by E! News, Madison claimed she had suffered from depression and suicidal thoughts, and that Hefner’s accusations of infidelity ultimately led to their split.

Who is Holly Madison’s ex-husband?

Madison was married to music entrepreneur Pasquale Rotella from 2013 to 2019. According to his profile, he is the founder and creator of the world’s largest dance music and experience company, Insomniac, and is also an experience creator, businessman, philanthropist and film producer.

How many children does Holly Madison have?

Madison and Rotella share two children – daughter Rainbow and son Forest. They welcomed them in March 2013 and August 2016, respectively, according to People. It seems the former lovers have made it work for their children when it comes to co-parenting too. “I’m really lucky to be co-parenting with somebody who also understands that all they want is what’s best for the kids,” the Girls Next Door alum told Us Weekly in 2023.
