Travis Kelce 'could spend $1 million on an engagement ring for Taylor Swift', expert says

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce are the celebrity couple of the moment and if he decides to buy her an engagement ring, it could come with a hefty price tag.

Taylor Swift and her NBA-playing beau have been together publicly since September after she attended a Kansas City Chiefs game. Since then, she and Travis Kelce have gone from strength to strength and have often delighted Swifties with a lot of PDA, including their ‘fairytale’ celebratory kiss after his team won at the Super Bowl. It wouldn’t surprise us if Travis decided to get down on one knee soon – with a very expensive ring.

Travis Kelce could gift Taylor Swift a $1 million engagement ring – data suggests

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce have been sending fans into meltdown over a possible engagement and he just might buy a ring that could put a bit of a dent in his net worth.

According to Buzz Bingo, who analyzed famous faces and their net worths, they tend to buy rings costing 2.59% of their assets.

If this were the case for Travis, the 34-year-old would spend a whopping $1,044,029 on the music star. Travis is currently worth $40 million but that could all change. After the Super Bowl, he is said to be getting quite a big bonus. Rumors say the celebrity could earn an extra $337,000. This means there’s a bit more wriggle room for the star to spend a bit more on his love, though it would be 2.65% of his current worth.

Traylor has an estimated combined net worth of $1,144,869,960 meaning her net worth contributes 97% to the celebrity couple’s total.

The only couple above them are Beyoncé and Jay-Z. Bey’s engagement ring cost $6 million and their combined net worth is $1.8 billion so the percentage was significantly lower at 0.45%.

Traylor could be engaged ‘sooner than expected’

Despite the rumor Travis Kelce gave Taylor Swift an engagement ring last New Year’s, they haven’t responded to the claims they’re looking to get hitched. Even though a jewler offered to make Travis Kelce a $1m engagement ring for Taylor, there are no signs he took up the offer.

Other reports think they’ll be engaged to be married by the end of the year is highly unlikely. But a celebrity psychic told us it could be sooner rather than later. Adam Norton explained to Celebrity Tidbit exclusively he “absolutely sees” the couple taking the next big step.

“Travis [Kelce] and Taylor [Swift] I see working really well! An engagement I absolutely see on the cards, and it’s not too far away either!” Norton told us, and he’s predicted when it will happen.

“I keep seeing the night sky so potentially they’ll get engaged during the evening or a ring picture will be taken and put on social media during the evening…”

He continued: “But their relationship I do feel will continue to blossom into 2024. August / September and November / December will be very significant to Taylor and Travis’ relationship.
