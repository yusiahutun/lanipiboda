How to use American Express gift card on Amazon to shop

May 2024 · 7 minute read

If you are an American Express customer, you have probably received the American Express Gift Card. An American Express Gift Card is a prepaid payment card you can use for online or in-store shopping immediately after purchasing an item. You can use the card anywhere it is accepted in the United States, including select online stores like Amazon. But do you know how to use American Express gift card on Amazon to shop?

Amazon offers the most convenient platform to spend money on your American Express Gift Card. But before you can determine how to use an American Express gift card on Amazon to shop, the first step is to activate the gift card if you have received it for the first time.

You will have to add the card to your Amazon account. Once done, you can make purchases and track your spending. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use American Gift Card on Amazon.

Does Amazon accept American Express?

Yes, Amazon accepts American Express as a payment method. You can use your American Express credit card to pay for an order on Amazon. Also, Amazon accepts American Express gift cards and allows you to use at least one American Express Membership Rewards point.

How to activate American Express Gift Card

First, you must activate your American Express Gift Card to use it on any e-commerce platform, including Amazon. However, if you purchased the Card via AmexGiftCard.com, you can use the card immediately. Otherwise, here is what to do:

  • Go to balance.amexgiftcard.com and sign in.
  • Enter your card details to activate.
  • Alternatively, you can call the toll-free number on the back of the card.
  • Ensure to provide the card number, valid thru date, and security code on the back of the card.

Once you activate the card, it is ready to use.

How to add American Express Gift Card to Amazon

Your activated American Express Gift Card is ready to use at any online or local store. But on Amazon, you must add the card to your account before proceeding. Follow the steps below.

  • Log in to your Amazon account.
  • Click on the Account & Lists drop-down menu.
  • Go to the Account tab.
  • Select Your Payments.
  • Go to Add a payment method.
  • Add Your American Express Gift Card as a debit or credit card.

When adding the American Express Gift Card, do not add it as a gift card. Instead, add it as a debit or credit card. Then, finally, check your Gift Card balance to start using it on Amazon.

How to use American Express Gift Card on Amazon

Before you can use an American Express gift card online, especially on Amazon, the first thing is to check your balance. In other words, the balance on the gift card should be more than the entire purchase cost, including taxes.

Some purchases on Amazon require a hold of up to 20% above the product amount. Therefore, you must put this into consideration before proceeding with your purchase. Otherwise, the purchase will not go through. In addition, if Amazon authorises for an amount greater than the actual purchase, American Express will temporarily decrease the available balance on your card by the authorisation amount.

Can you use American Express Gift Card if its balance does not cover the purchase price on Amazon? Generally, Amazon’s policy does not allow the use of multiple payment options to cover the total purchase price of an item. Therefore, if the balance in your American Express Gift Card does not cover the purchase price, you cannot use another form of payment to make the purchase.

When you are ready to make a payment, select Credit or Debit as the payment method. Avoid choosing a Gift Card because it will not go through. Another way of using the Amex Gift Card is to reload it to Amazon.

How to reload Amex Gift Card to Amazon

You can load your Amex Gift Card balance into your Amazon account to make purchases. Here is what to do:

  • Log in to your Amazon account.
  • Go to the top menu and select Gift Cards.
  • On the new page, click on Reload Your Balance.
  • Enter the amount you want to add to your gift card.
  • Hit Buy Now.
  • If prompted, log in to your Amazon account.
  • Select Add a credit or debit card.
  • Enter your Amex Gift Card information.
  • Press Next.
  • Select the Gift Card as your new payment method.
  • Click on Done.
  • Confirm your gift card information and click on Reload.

Your Amazon account now has funds from your Amex gift Card. You can use it to buy items on Amazon.

Can you use American Express Gift Card to purchase an Amazon Gift Card?

Besides using an Amex Gift Card as your payment option on Amazon, you can convert it into an Amazon Gift Card. The method has an advantage for users with small balances in their Amex gift cards. Also, this is the only option where Amazon accepts multiple payment options. In other words, if your primary payment method has a lower balance, you can top it up with your Amazon Gift Card balance.

However, converting your Amex gift card balance to an Amazon gift card is a sophisticated process and requires you to use a third-party service. Here is what you can do.

  • Log in to your Amazon account.
  • Go to the search bar.
  • Using the search window, type in DoorDash gift card email delivery.
  • Scroll down to the Enter amount section.
  • Type in the amount left on your Amex Gift Card.
  • Provide your email address in the TO section.
  • Choose Now in the Delivery Date section.
  • Click on the Buy Now button.
  • Under Payment Method, select your Amex Gift Card.
  • Amazon will send you an email with the Amazon Gift Card code.

After receiving the code, the next step is to load it into your Amazon account.

  • Log in to your Amazon account if prompted.
  • In the upper menu, select Redeem a Gift Card.
  • In the next window, type in the claim code of your gift card.
  • Click on Apply to your balance.

How do I redeem my Amex e-voucher on Amazon?

If you have an Amex e-voucher and you want to redeem it on Amazon, here is what to do:

  • First, log into your Amazon account.
  • Next, click on the Accounts & Lists icon at the top right of the page.
  • Select Your Vouchers from the drop-down menu.
  • Enter the voucher code into the Redemption Code box.
  • Click on Apply Voucher.

The voucher amount will reduce from your total once you make a purchase.
