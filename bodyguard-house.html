Bodyguard Home for Sale - Cabin From the Bodyguard

April 2024 · 2 minute read

If you ever wanted to own a piece of Hollywood history, now's your chance. The rustic South Lake Tahoe cabin featured in 1992's The Bodyguard and 1998's City of Angels is on the market.

The iconic California home was designed in 1969 by Paul Hamilton, who was inspired by the work of Frank Lloyd Wright. The five-bedroom, three-bath, 3,000-square-foot home stuns with custom wood furnishings throughout. In fact, there isn't an inch of drywall in the house; it's all wood.

Peter Tye, 2view Media LLC

Located about a mile from Lake Tahoe, the retreat, situated on 2.74 acres, also comes with breathtaking views of Fallen Leaf Lake, the surrounding mountains, and lush greenery.

In The Bodyguard, you may recall the scene where Kevin Coster's character jumps off the home's pier to save a boy from a boat that's about to explode.

Peter Tye, 2view Media LLC

In City of Angels, the house served as Meg Ryan's uncle's cabin.

The famous home still has all the rustic charm you remember from the movies—it's listed for $6,750,000 by Doug Rosner of Chase International South Tahoe Realty; you can find more information on Zillow.
