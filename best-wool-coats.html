19 Best Wool Coats for Women to Wear in 2024 and Beyond

June 2024 · 11 minute read

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Valentina Valdinoci

Best Everyday Wool Coat

AYR The Closer

Best Everyday Wool Coat

AYR The Closer

Credit: AYR

This coat sums up everything that keeps me coming back to AYR for everyday classics. The Closer is a single-breasted belted wrap coat made from a top-notch Italian wool that looks great on top of casual errand outfits and wedding guest dresses alike. When you’re in danger of overthinking your winter outerwear, it’s the coat you know will look good regardless of how you style it.

Sizes: XS–XL

Colors: Camel, shiitake heather, navy

What reviewers are saying: “Initially I was concerned that the package was so light but once I put it on I fell in love with the coat. The Navy color is dark and beautiful. The quality of the material and cut is impressive. I bought a small since I always wear small and there is room for a big fluffy sweater. I never spent this much on a coat before so I was ready to return it if I wasn’t sure of it. I love it.”

Best Oversize Wool Coat

The Row Dhanila Coat

Best Oversize Wool Coat

The Row Dhanila Coat

Credit: The Row

When I think of a big, sexy fall coat, the Row is exactly the label that comes to mind. Its oversize coats are a highlight of the fall and winter collections each year. For 2023, all eyes are on this double-breasted wool coat with exaggerated shoulders and long sleeves.

Sizes: XS–L

Colors: Navy

Best Reversible Wool Coat

Lafayette 148 Wool-Cashmere Double-Face Reversible Oversized Coat

Best Reversible Wool Coat

Lafayette 148 Wool-Cashmere Double-Face Reversible Oversized Coat

Credit: Lafayette 148

This clean minimalist wool coat has more than meets the eye. For days when you want to stay neutral, the gray side with a high-neck fastening can go on the outside. Then when you’re feeling more colorful, the coat reverses to a stunning iris blue. The entire coat is made from a mix of wool and cashmere for the softest, coziest feeling.

Sizes: XXS–XXL, 1X–3X

Colors: Gray/blue

Best Wool Coat Under $100

Uniqlo x Clare Waight Keller Double-Face Wrap Coat

Best Wool Coat Under $100

Uniqlo x Clare Waight Keller Double-Face Wrap Coat

Credit: Uniqlo

As part of one of fall 2023’s most exciting collaborations, former Givenchy creative director Clare Waight Keller designed an open-faced wool coat that looks so much more expensive than it is. It’s a collarless design that can be layered like a cardigan, yet it’s just as warm as a standard wool coat.

Sizes: XXS–XL

Colors: Navy, gray, yellow

What reviewers are saying: “I fell in love with the coat the moment I tried it on. The sleeves are amazing… roomy enough to look chic and accommodate layers underneath yet not big enough to wear you down (even if you have a petite frame). More than anything, I love the flow of the coat. It moves beautifully as you walk. Seems slightly kimono-inspired while still looking modern and sharp. It’s sophisticated and bohemian in equal parts to me. Looks best with a flared long/longish skirt.”

Best Wool Car Coat

Skall Studio Mary Coat

Best Wool Car Coat

Skall Studio Mary Coat

Now 30% Off

Credit: Farfetch

Add the eternal shape of a single-button car coat to a warm wool check fabric, and you land on a wool coat that will last for decades. This style from Skall Studio, one of my favorite brands from Copenhagen Fashion Week, encapsulates just why Scandi brands are so revered for their outerwear. The shape is practical enough to wear all over the city—even on a bike—but the overall look is timeless. Personally, I’d love to channel Skall Studio’s runway styling and tie a lace or crochet scarf on top.

Sizes: 34–40 FR

Colors: Black check

Best Wool Blazer Coat

& Other Stories Relaxed Double-Breasted Wool Coat

Best Wool Blazer Coat

& Other Stories Relaxed Double-Breasted Wool Coat

Now 59% Off

Credit: & Other Stories

Oversize blazers and straight-leg jeans are my most-repeated outfit formula. Chic may be an overused word, but this jacket is exactly that. It uses the classic codes of suiting, but it doesn’t look like it’s trying too hard.

Best Camel Wool Coat

Best Camel Wool Coat

What reviewers are saying: “This is a beautiful coat! I purchased for a trip up north and I wasn’t disappointed. […] Ordered my usual size and it fit perfectly. Also [received] a number of compliments from strangers? Always a reminder that it was a super purchase.”

Best Celebrity-Approved Wool Coat

Mackage Mai Long Wool Wrap Coat

Best Celebrity-Approved Wool Coat

Mackage Mai Long Wool Wrap Coat

Credit: Nordstrom

It’s been a few years since Meghan Markle first wore a version of Mackage’s waterfall wrap coat. Many special appearances and feverishly tracked outfits later, this coat style is the first that comes to mind when I think of the best wool coat with Markle’s endorsement. The 2023 update comes in cream, black, and sand, all with a draping fold-over neckline and instant royal-adjacent elegance. Sizes: XXS–XL

Colors: Cream, black, gray, sand

What reviewers are saying: “I bought this in sand and it is the most beautiful coat. It’s lightweight and luxe. Dress it up or down. It’s long. I bought a medium and I’m 5'6" and it’s below mid calf, but it’s beautiful belted or worn open. I would highly recommend.”

Best Wool Barn Coat

Best Wool Barn Coat
