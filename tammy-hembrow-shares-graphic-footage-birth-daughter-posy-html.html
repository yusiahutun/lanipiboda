Tammy Hembrow shares graphic footage of birth to daughter Posy

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Tammy Hembrow has shared graphic footage giving birth to her newborn daughter Posy.

The fitness influencer, 28, posted a raw 10-minute video to her YouTube channel on Tuesday and issued a trigger warning to her fans, writing: 'This is my real and raw experience with a lot of screaming.'

In the video, Tammy can be seen being supported by her sisters Amy and Emilee as well as her fiancé Matt Poole after arriving to the hospital. 

Tammy Hembrow (pictured) issued a trigger warning as she shared graphic footage giving birth to daughter Posy on Tuesday

She was then showered down by Amy, while leaning onto Matt screaming in pain.

Moments later, Tammy's sisters attached a TENS machine - designed to assist with pain - to her back.
